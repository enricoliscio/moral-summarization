SUMMARY: House Intelligence Committee Chairman Adam Schiff aggressively defended himself against calls for his resignation from President Trump and Republicans, citing contacts between the Trump campaign and Russia. Schiff listed instances of the Trump campaign's interactions with Russia, stating that he found them unacceptable and that they damaged the integrity of the panel. Rep. Mike Conaway, a senior member of the panel, called for Schiff's resignation, accusing him of promoting a false narrative. Trump also demanded Schiff's resignation via Twitter, while Schiff reiterated his plans to continue the investigation into Trump's links to Russia. The hearing saw further interruptions from Republican members expressing dissatisfaction with Schiff, with Rep. Michael Turner comparing him to Joseph McCarthy. Democrats defended Schiff, calling the GOP attack an "ambush" and a "cheap shot."
END OF SUMMARY.
