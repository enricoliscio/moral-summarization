SUMMARY: Former US Defense Secretary James Mattis has publicly criticized President Donald Trump for his response to the protests following the death of George Floyd. Mattis, who resigned from his position in 2018, writes that Trump is deliberately dividing the American people and that his actions threaten the American experiment. He condemns the use of the military to suppress protests and the violation of Constitutional rights, contrasting the American ethos of unity with Nazi ideology. Mattis also criticizes the current Secretary of Defense, Mark Esper, and other senior officials for their role in the response to the protests. He emphasizes the importance of civilian leadership in maintaining public order and the need to respect the apolitical nature of the military. Mattis' statement highlights his belief that Trump's actions are a direct threat to the foundations of American democracy.
END OF SUMMARY.
