SUMMARY: Former Secretary of Defense James Mattis has released a statement strongly criticizing President Donald Trump's response to the recent protests and his overall leadership. Mattis emphasizes the importance of unity in the American ethos and contrasts it with Nazi ideology. He highlights the threat Trump's actions pose to the American experiment and criticizes the use of the military to suppress protests and create a photo opportunity for the president. Mattis also expresses his dissatisfaction with the current Secretary of Defense, Mark Esper, and other senior officials. He emphasizes the need for civilian leadership in maintaining public order and the importance of preserving the trusted bond between the military and civilian society. Mattis' statement marks a shift from his previous stance of remaining silent about the administration, indicating that he now feels the need to speak out due to the gravity of the situation.
