SUMMARY: In a powerful statement, former Secretary of Defense James Mattis condemns President Donald Trump's response to the police killing of George Floyd and the ensuing protests. Mattis emphasizes the importance of uniting the American people, contrasting it with Trump's attempts to divide us. He highlights the lack of mature leadership and the threat to the American experiment. Mattis criticizes the use of the military to dominate civilian society, eroding the moral bond between the military and the people they are sworn to protect. He emphasizes that keeping public order rests with civilian leaders who understand their communities. Mattis' statement is a strong defense of American values and a rejection of Trump's divisive and destructive actions. It is a call to action to unite and protect our country, confident that we are better than our politics.

END OF SUMMARY.
