SUMMARY: In a powerful statement, former US Defense Secretary James Mattis strongly condemns President Donald Trump's leadership, stating that Trump deliberately tries to divide the American people instead of uniting them. Mattis highlights the contrast between American values of unity and Nazi ideology, emphasizing the importance of unity in overcoming the current crisis. He expresses his dissatisfaction with Trump's response to the police killing of George Floyd and the subsequent protests, specifically criticizing the use of the military to dominate US cities and violate the constitutional rights of citizens. Mattis also implicitly criticizes the current Secretary of Defense, Mark Esper, and other senior officials, emphasizing the need for civilian leadership in maintaining public order. Mattis' statement reflects his belief that Trump's actions directly threaten the American experiment and the values it stands for.
