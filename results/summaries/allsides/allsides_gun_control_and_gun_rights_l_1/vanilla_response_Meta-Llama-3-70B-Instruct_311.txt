SUMMARY:

The article is a personal reflection by a mayor who has dealt with the aftermath of mass shootings in their community. They express frustration with the lack of leadership from Washington to address the issue of gun violence and instead, mayors from across the political spectrum are taking action themselves. The mayor highlights the importance of empowering young people to demand change and take action to prevent gun violence. The mayor mentions their own Youth Council to End Gun Violence, which advises on issues of gun safety and engages young people in the fight against gun violence.
