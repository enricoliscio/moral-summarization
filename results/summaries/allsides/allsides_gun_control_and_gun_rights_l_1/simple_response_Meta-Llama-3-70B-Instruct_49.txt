The author, a mayor, reflects on the devastating impact of mass shootings on communities and the moral responsibility of leaders to take action. The author's great-grandmother fled anti-Semitic violence, and their grandfather was brought to the US as an infant, highlighting the importance of protecting immigrants and promoting tolerance. The author's Youth Council to End Gun Violence, comprised of young leaders, is working to create a safer future.
