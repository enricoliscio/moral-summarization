SUMMARY:

In a heroic display of bravery, two volunteer security team members at the West Freeway Church of Christ in Texas drew their weapons and confronted a gunman who opened fire during a Sunday service, killing him and saving countless lives. The quick actions of the security team, praised by authorities as "heroic," brought an end to the attack that could have been much worse. Two people were killed in the attack, and the gunman, who had roots in the area, was also fatally shot. The incident highlights the importance of responsible gun ownership and the need for security measures in places of worship. Authorities have hailed the state's gun laws, which allowed the licensed handgun owners to carry loaded weapons into the church. The tragic incident serves as a reminder of the importance of vigilance and preparedness in the face of evil acts of violence.

END OF SUMMARY.
