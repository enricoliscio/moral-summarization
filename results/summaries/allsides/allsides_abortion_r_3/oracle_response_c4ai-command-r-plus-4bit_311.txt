SUMMARY: Democrats are intensifying their efforts to safeguard abortion access in response to recent restrictive laws passed by several states. Senator Cory Booker has pledged to establish a "White House Office of Reproductive Freedom" and has proposed comprehensive plans to protect abortion rights through executive and congressional action. He aims to fund abortions overseas and ensure that Roe v. Wade is codified into federal law. Senator Kristen Gillibrand argues that restricting abortion access is "against Christian faith" and the Constitution, emphasizing the separation of church and state. She, along with other candidates, supports enshrining abortion access into federal law. Joe Biden has also expressed support for legislative action to protect abortion rights. Democratic lawmakers are actively working to protect abortion access through the "Women's Health Protection Act," underscoring the urgency of the situation and the need to safeguard reproductive rights and women's lives from danger and risk. 

END OF SUMMARY.
