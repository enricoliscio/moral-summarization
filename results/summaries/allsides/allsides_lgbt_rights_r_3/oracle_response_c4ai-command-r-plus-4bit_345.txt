SUMMARY: NFL star quarterback Drew Brees was heavily criticized by the LGBTQ lobby after expressing support for a program that encourages children to bring their Bibles to school and openly live their Christian faith. In a promotional video, Brees shared his favorite Bible scripture and urged kids to share their faith with others. However, his actions were met with backlash, with some accusing him of aligning with a "known racist discriminatory religious cult" and supporting gay conversion therapy. Brees defended himself, stating that he only encouraged kids to bring their Bibles to school and that he does not support discrimination or inequality. He affirmed his belief in loving, respecting, and accepting all people. Brees expressed confusion about the negative response to his actions and denied supporting any discriminatory agendas.
