SUMMARY: NFL star Drew Brees faced backlash from LGBTQ advocates after promoting a program encouraging children to bring their Bibles to school and openly practice their Christian faith. In a video, Brees referenced a Bible scripture and urged kids to discuss their faith with peers. His actions were criticized by some as supporting an "anti-gay religious cult" that promotes conversion therapy and opposes anti-discrimination laws. Brees responded to the controversy, denying support for discriminatory groups and stating his belief in loving, respecting, and accepting all people. He clarified that his intention was to encourage children to bring their Bibles to school and expressed confusion over the negative response. The incident sparked debates about the intersection of religion and LGBTQ rights, with Brees' stance attracting both praise and criticism.
