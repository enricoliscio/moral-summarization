SUMMARY: NFL star Drew Brees faced backlash from LGBTQ advocates for his participation in a program encouraging children to bring their Bibles to school and openly practice their Christian faith. In a promotional video, Brees shared his favorite Bible scripture and urged kids to discuss their faith. Critics, including Robert Littal and Deadspin, accused Brees of aligning with a discriminatory anti-LGBTQ religious cult that promotes conversion therapy. Brees responded on Instagram, denying support for any discriminatory groups and stating that he believes in loving, respecting, and accepting all people. He expressed confusion about the negative response to his video, which he said was simply about encouraging kids to bring their Bibles to school. Brees emphasized his stance against inequality and hoped his statement would set the record straight.
END OF SUMMARY.
