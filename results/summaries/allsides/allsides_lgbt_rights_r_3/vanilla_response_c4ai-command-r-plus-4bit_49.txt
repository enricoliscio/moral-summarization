SUMMARY: NFL quarterback Drew Brees faced backlash from LGBT advocates and their allies for participating in a program that encourages children to bring their Bibles to school and openly practice their Christian faith. Brees, a devout Christian, promoted the event in a video where he shared his favorite Bible scripture and encouraged students to discuss their faith with peers. LGBT activists and media outlets criticized Brees, accusing him of aligning with a discriminatory and anti-gay religious group that promotes conversion therapy. Brees responded to the accusations on Instagram, denying support for any discriminatory groups and affirming his belief in loving, respecting, and accepting all people. He expressed confusion about the negative response to his video, as his intention was to encourage students to openly practice their Christian faith. Brees emphasized that he does not condone inequality or discrimination and stated that he wanted to set the record straight and move forward.
END OF SUMMARY.
