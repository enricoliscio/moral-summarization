Jurek's radical views align with antifa, and he suggests that violence is the only language fascists understand. The video raises questions about the true nature of the Sanders campaign and whether Jurek's views are representative of the campaign's ideology. The Sanders campaign has not commented on the video. The revelations are deeply concerning and have moral implications, highlighting the dangers of radical ideologies and the importance of promoting peaceful and respectful political discourse.
