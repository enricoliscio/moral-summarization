SUMMARY:

President Trump addressed the nation after the Las Vegas attack, calling it an "act of pure evil" that left 58 people dead and 515 hospitalized. He announced a visit to Las Vegas to meet with law enforcement, first responders, and families of victims. In a unifying speech, Trump offered prayers and comfort, citing scripture to remind Americans that God is close to the broken-hearted. He emphasized the importance of unity, faith, family, and shared values in the face of evil, saying that "our unity cannot be shattered by evil." Trump also expressed hope that even in darkness, a single ray of hope can illuminate the way. He and the First Lady prayed for the nation, seeking a day when evil is banished and the innocent are safe from hatred and fear.

END OF SUMMARY.
