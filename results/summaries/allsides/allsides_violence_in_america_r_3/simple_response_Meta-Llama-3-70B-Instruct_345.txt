SUMMARY: President Trump condemned the Las Vegas attack as an "act of pure evil" that left 58 people dead and 515 hospitalized. In a somber address, he offered prayers and condolences to the victims' families, quoting scripture to emphasize the importance of unity and faith in the face of darkness. Trump emphasized that "our unity cannot be shattered by evil" and that even in the darkest times, hope can illuminate the way. He also announced plans to visit Las Vegas to meet with law enforcement, first responders, and victims' families. The president's speech was a call to national unity and a reminder that evil cannot break the bonds that unite Americans. END OF SUMMARY.
