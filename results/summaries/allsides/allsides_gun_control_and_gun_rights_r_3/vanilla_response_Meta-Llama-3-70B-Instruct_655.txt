SUMMARY:

A male student was taken into custody after a shooting at Noblesville West Middle School in Indiana, leaving a student and a teacher critically injured. The suspect, who has not been identified, allegedly excused himself from class and returned with two handguns. A science teacher, identified as Jason Seaman, reportedly tackled the student, saving others from potential harm. The teacher was shot multiple times and underwent surgery. Authorities have opened an investigation into the incident, with multiple agencies, including the FBI and ATF, involved. The school does not have metal detectors, but a resource officer was present at the time of the incident. Politicians, including Vice President Mike Pence, and the Indianapolis Colts have expressed their condolences and support for the victims and the community. The incident comes just a week after a similar attack at a high school in Texas.

END OF SUMMARY.
