SUMMARY:

A tragic shooting occurred at Noblesville West Middle School in Indiana, leaving a student and a teacher critically injured. A male student was taken into custody after the incident. Authorities praised the swift response of law enforcement and first responders. A heroic science teacher, Jason Seaman, reportedly tackled the suspect, saving countless lives. The teacher was shot multiple times and underwent surgery. Politicians, including Vice President Mike Pence, and local leaders expressed their condolences and support for the community. The Indiana State Teachers Association condemned the incident, asking "When is enough, enough?" in reference to the national crisis of school violence. The incident comes just a week after a similar attack in Texas, highlighting the urgent need for action to keep schools safe from violence.

END OF SUMMARY.
