SUMMARY:

A tragic shooting occurred at Noblesville West Middle School in Indiana, leaving a student and a teacher critically injured. A male student was taken into custody after the incident. Authorities praised the swift response of law enforcement and first responders, and a heroic science teacher, Jason Seaman, who tackled the suspect and was shot multiple times. The teacher's bravery likely prevented further injuries. The incident has sparked outrage and calls for action to prevent such tragedies from happening again. Politicians, including Vice President Mike Pence, and the Indiana State Teachers Association have expressed their condolences and frustration, with the latter asking "When is enough, enough?" The shooting comes just a week after a similar incident in Santa Fe, Texas, highlighting the urgent need for solutions to address school violence.

END OF SUMMARY.
