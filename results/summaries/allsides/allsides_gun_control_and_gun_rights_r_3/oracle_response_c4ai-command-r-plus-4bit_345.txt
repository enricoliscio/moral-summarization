SUMMARY: A male student was taken into custody after a shooting incident at an Indiana middle school that left a teacher and a student critically injured. Authorities have not identified the suspect or victims but confirmed that the situation was resolved quickly. A student reported that a science teacher tackled the shooter and swatted a gun out of his hand, preventing further injuries. The teacher, Jason Seaman, was shot multiple times and underwent surgery. Politicians, including Vice President Mike Pence, expressed their thoughts and prayers for the victims and the community. The shooting comes a week after a tragic attack at a Texas high school that killed ten people. Indiana's governor, Eric Holcomb, stated that their thoughts are with those affected by this horrible situation and that state leaders are monitoring the investigation. Noblesville, the town where the shooting occurred, is about 20 miles from Indianapolis and the middle school has about 1,300 students. Authorities are investigating the incident and are working to ensure the safety of the community.
END OF SUMMARY.
