SUMMARY:

A tragic shooting incident occurred at Noblesville West Middle School in Indiana, leaving a student and a teacher critically injured. Authorities took a male student into custody after responding to a call about an active shooter. The victims were rushed to the hospital, and their families were notified. The suspect and victims have not been identified. The school does not have metal detectors, but a resource officer was present during the incident. A heroic science teacher, Jason Seaman, reportedly tackled the student who fired shots, saving many lives. Politicians, including Vice President Mike Pence, tweeted their thoughts and prayers for the victims and the community. The Indiana State Teachers Association released a statement condemning the national crisis of school violence, asking "When is enough, enough?" The incident comes a week after a similar attack in Santa Fe, Texas, that killed eight students and two teachers.

END OF SUMMARY.
