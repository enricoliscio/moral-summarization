SUMMARY:

A male student was taken into custody after a shooting at Noblesville West Middle School in Indiana, leaving a student and a teacher critically injured. The suspect, who has not been identified, allegedly excused himself from class and returned with two handguns. A science teacher, identified as Jason Seaman, reportedly tackled the student, preventing further harm. The teacher was shot multiple times and underwent surgery. The school does not have metal detectors, but a resource officer was present during the incident. Authorities have opened an investigation into the shooting, which is believed to have resolved quickly. Politicians, including Vice President Mike Pence, and local officials have expressed their condolences and support for the victims and the community. The incident comes a week after a similar attack at a high school in Texas.

END OF SUMMARY.
