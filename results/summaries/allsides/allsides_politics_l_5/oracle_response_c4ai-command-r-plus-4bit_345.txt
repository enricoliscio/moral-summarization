SUMMARY: Alabama Republican Senate candidate Roy Moore has threatened to sue the Washington Post over an article that alleged he pursued sexual relationships with teenagers, including a 14-year-old girl when he was in his 30s. Moore denied the allegations, calling them "false and untrue attacks" on his character and reputation, and stating that the incidents described in the article never happened. The Washington Post report was based on interviews with over 30 people and included allegations of sexual misconduct and inappropriate touching by Moore. Moore has denied any wrongdoing and framed the article as a politically motivated attack, promising "revelations" about the story in the coming days. The story has caused a rift in the Republican Party, with some withdrawing their support for Moore in the upcoming election. The allegations have sparked a debate about the protection of young people and the moral conduct expected of public figures.
END OF SUMMARY.
