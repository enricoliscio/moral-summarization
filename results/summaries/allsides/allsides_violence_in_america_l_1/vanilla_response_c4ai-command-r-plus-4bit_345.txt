SUMMARY: One man was killed in Louisville after police and the National Guard opened fire on a group gathered in a parking lot. The incident occurred around 12:15 am when law enforcement tried to disperse the crowd, and someone in the group fired at them, leading to the fatal shooting. The Kentucky State Police have been authorized to independently investigate the incident. Louisville has been the site of destructive protests demanding justice for Breonna Taylor, an unarmed African American woman killed by police in March. Police Chief Steve Conrad acknowledged the lack of trust between law enforcement and the community, stating it will be an issue to work on for a long time. The investigation into the fatal shooting is ongoing, with video footage being collected and several witnesses interviewed. The incident further inflames tensions in Louisville, where protests against racial injustice and police brutality have been ongoing.
END OF SUMMARY.
