According to authorities, someone in the crowd fired at officers, prompting the National Guard and Louisville police to return fire, resulting in the killing of one man. The police chief acknowledged that many people do not trust the police, which is an issue that needs to be addressed. The investigation into the fatal shooting is ongoing, with police interviewing witnesses and collecting video footage. The incident is likely to further inflame tensions in Louisville, where protesters have been demanding justice for the unarmed Breonna Taylor, who died when police broke down her door and returned fire. The city is grappling with issues of trust, justice, and accountability in the wake of these tragic events.
