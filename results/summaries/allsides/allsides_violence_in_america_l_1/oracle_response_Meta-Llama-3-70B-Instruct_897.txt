SUMMARY:

A fatal shooting occurred in Louisville when police and the National Guard opened fire on a crowd in a parking lot, killing one man. The incident followed a night of destructive protests over the deaths of Breonna Taylor and George Floyd. According to authorities, someone in the crowd fired at officers, prompting them to return fire. The shooting is likely to further inflame tensions in Louisville, where protesters have been demanding justice for Taylor, an unarmed African American EMT who was killed in her apartment by police on March 13. The police chief acknowledged that many people do not trust the police, and that this issue needs to be addressed. The incident highlights the need for justice and accountability in the face of police violence. The authorities are investigating the shooting and collecting video footage, which will be released to the public soon.

END OF SUMMARY.
