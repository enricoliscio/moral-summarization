SUMMARY:

A 17-year-old boy, Dimitrios Pagourtzis, has been charged with fatally shooting 10 people, including 8 students and 2 teachers, at a high school in Santa Fe, Texas. According to the mother of one of the victims, Shana Fisher, Pagourtzis had made aggressive advances towards her daughter, who rejected him and embarrassed him in class. A week later, he allegedly opened fire on his classmates. Authorities have not yet released a motive for the massacre, but Pagourtzis has confessed to the crime. The school district has denied claims that Pagourtzis was bullied, including by a football coach. The community is grieving, with many attending vigils and services to mourn the victims. The shooting has reignited the national debate over gun ownership, with some calling for stricter gun control laws and others advocating for increased security measures in schools.

END OF SUMMARY.
