SUMMARY:

A 17-year-old boy, Dimitrios Pagourtzis, has been charged with fatally shooting 10 people, including 8 students and 2 teachers, at a high school in Santa Fe, Texas. According to the mother of one of the victims, Shana Fisher, 16, Pagourtzis had made aggressive advances towards her daughter, who rejected him and embarrassed him in class. A week later, he allegedly carried out the massacre. The motive behind the shooting is still unclear, but authorities have denied reports that Pagourtzis was bullied. The suspect has confessed to the crime and is currently in jail. The community is in mourning, with many attending vigils and services to pay tribute to the victims. The incident has reignited the national debate over gun ownership, with some calling for stricter gun control laws.
