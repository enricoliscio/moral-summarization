SUMMARY: Law enforcement serves as a reminder to society that legality is essential for civilization. Deterrence, the prompt and uniform enforcement of the law, is crucial in maintaining order and preventing violations. The article discusses how the lack of enforcement of certain laws, such as immigration laws and campus protests, can lead to a breakdown of deterrence and encourage further lawlessness. It argues that the presence of millions of undocumented immigrants and the lack of consequences for violent campus protests send a message that laws can be ignored. The article also highlights the threat of violence against public figures, including the President, and the perceived lack of consequences for those making such threats. It suggests that the selective enforcement of laws based on political ideologies can lead to a dangerous slippery slope and undermine the rule of law. The piece concludes by emphasizing the importance of law enforcement in maintaining order and preventing anarchy.
END OF SUMMARY.
