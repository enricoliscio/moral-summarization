SUMMARY:

In a bold move to tackle America's bitter gun control divide, prominent Democrats are set to introduce sweeping legislation to impose background checks on all gun sales. Senators Chris Murphy and Richard Blumenthal, who represent Connecticut where the Sandy Hook elementary school tragedy occurred, will lead the demands for fresh action. The newly empowered Democratic majority in the House of Representatives is expected to prioritize this legislation in the incoming Congress of 2019. The move comes after years of inaction from the White House and Congress, despite pledges from Donald Trump to curb gun violence. Family members of victims from recent mass shootings will join the call to action, demanding change and highlighting the devastating consequences of lax gun control. The moral imperative is clear: it's time for Washington to take decisive action to prevent further tragedies and protect American lives.

END OF SUMMARY.
