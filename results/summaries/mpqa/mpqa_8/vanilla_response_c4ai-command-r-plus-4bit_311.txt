SUMMARY: In a statement, Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso said that Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez's main challenge is to restore normalcy after the recent unrest. Cardoso emphasized the importance of open communication and social harmony. Chavez assured that he would not seek retribution against military members who opposed him. Jimmy Carter, ex-US President, expressed support for Chavez's plan for dialogue and intends to visit Venezuela soon.

END OF SUMMARY.
