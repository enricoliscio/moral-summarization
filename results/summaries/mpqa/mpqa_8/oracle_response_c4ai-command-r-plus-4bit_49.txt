SUMMARY: In a show of support for Venezuela's President Hugo Chavez, Brazil's President Fernando Henrique Cardoso emphasized the importance of restoring harmony and resolving the recent conflicts in Venezuela. President Chavez has pledged to avoid vengeance against those involved in the coup attempt, opting instead for dialogue and reconciliation. This approach has been praised by former U.S. President Jimmy Carter, who plans to visit Venezuela soon. President Cardoso believes that President Chavez's main challenge is to reestablish normalcy and open communication channels to achieve social harmony in Venezuela.
END OF SUMMARY.
