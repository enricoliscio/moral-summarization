SUMMARY: In a show of solidarity, President Fernando Henrique Cardoso of Brazil offers support to Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez in the aftermath of a coup attempt. Cardoso emphasizes the importance of restoring calm and opening lines of communication to achieve social harmony. Chavez assures that he will not seek vengeance on those involved in the coup, prioritizing national reconciliation. Former US President Jimmy Carter lends his support to Chavez's efforts, planning a visit to Venezuela to encourage peaceful dialogue. The challenge ahead lies in reestablishing stability and normalcy in Venezuela, with an emphasis on peaceful resolution and social cohesion.

END OF SUMMARY.
