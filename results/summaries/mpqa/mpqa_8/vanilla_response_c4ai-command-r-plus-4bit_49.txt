SUMMARY: Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso has stated that Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez's primary challenge is to restore normalcy after last week's unrest. Cardoso emphasized the importance of opening lines of communication and fostering social harmony. Chavez assured that he would not seek retribution against military personnel involved in the coup attempt. Former US President Jimmy Carter has expressed support for Chavez's commitment to dialogue and plans to visit Venezuela soon. Chavez's focus on reconciliation and dialogue is receiving international support as a means to move forward.

END OF SUMMARY.
