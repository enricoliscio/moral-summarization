SUMMARY: 
President Fernando Henrique Cardoso of Brazil has stated that the main challenge facing Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez is to reestablish normalcy after the recent conflicts. Cardoso emphasized the importance of opening communication channels to achieve social harmony in the country. In a positive development, Chavez has assured that he will not seek revenge against the military personnel who supported the failed coup attempt. This move has been endorsed by former US President Jimmy Carter, who will be visiting Venezuela soon to support the dialogue process. The focus on harmony and peaceful resolution is a welcome step towards resolving the crisis. By promoting dialogue and reconciliation, Chavez and Cardoso are working towards a more peaceful and stable future for Venezuela. 
END OF SUMMARY.
