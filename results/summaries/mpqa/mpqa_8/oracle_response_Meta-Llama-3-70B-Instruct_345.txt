SUMMARY: 
President Fernando Henrique Cardoso believes that the main challenge facing Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez is to reestablish normalcy after the recent conflicts. Cardoso emphasized the importance of opening communication channels to achieve social harmony in the country. In a positive development, Chavez has assured that he will not seek revenge against the military personnel who supported the failed coup attempt. This move is seen as a step towards reconciliation and harmony. Former US President Jimmy Carter has also expressed his support for Chavez's initiative to set up dialogue tables, and is scheduled to visit Venezuela soon. The focus on dialogue and reconciliation suggests a commitment to resolving conflicts peacefully and promoting social harmony. 

END OF SUMMARY.
