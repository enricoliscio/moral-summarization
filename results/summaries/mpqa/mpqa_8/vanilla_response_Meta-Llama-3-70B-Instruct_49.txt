SUMMARY:

Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso believes that Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez's main challenge is to reestablish normalcy in the country following last week's conflicts. Cardoso emphasized the importance of opening communication channels and achieving social harmony. Chavez has assured Cardoso that he does not intend to seek revenge against military personnel who supported the failed attempt to remove him from office. Former US President Jimmy Carter has expressed support for Chavez's promise to set up dialogue tables and is scheduled to visit Venezuela soon. The focus is now on restoring peace and stability in Venezuela, with international leaders urging Chavez to prioritize national reconciliation.

END OF SUMMARY.
