SUMMARY: In a show of support for Venezuela's President Hugo Chavez, President Fernando Henrique Cardoso of Brazil highlighted the importance of restoring peace and harmony in Venezuela after the recent conflicts. President Chavez has pledged to forgive those in the military who opposed him, opting for dialogue and reconciliation instead of revenge. This approach has been praised by former US President Jimmy Carter, who plans to visit Venezuela soon, as a positive step towards reestablishing normalcy and harmony in the country. President Cardoso's remarks emphasize the need for open communication and social harmony as key challenges for President Chavez in the aftermath of the recent unrest.
END OF SUMMARY.
