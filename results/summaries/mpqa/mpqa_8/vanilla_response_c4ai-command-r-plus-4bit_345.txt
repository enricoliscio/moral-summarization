SUMMARY: Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso has stated that Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez's main challenge is to restore normalcy after recent conflicts. Cardoso emphasized the importance of open communication and social harmony. Chavez assured that he will not seek revenge on military personnel involved in the coup attempt. Former US President Jimmy Carter supports Chavez's promise to initiate dialogue and plans to visit Venezuela soon. Chavez's focus on reconciliation and dialogue is receiving international support as Venezuela moves forward following the recent political crisis. END OF SUMMARY.
