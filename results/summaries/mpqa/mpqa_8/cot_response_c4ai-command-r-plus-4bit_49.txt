SUMMARY:
In a show of support for Venezuela's president Hugo Chavez, Brazil's President Fernando Henrique Cardoso highlighted the importance of reestablishing normalcy and social harmony in Venezuela following the recent conflicts. President Chavez has promised to open communication channels and refrain from seeking revenge against those involved in the coup attempt. This promise has been supported by former US President Jimmy Carter, who plans to visit Venezuela soon to encourage dialogue and peaceful resolution. President Cardoso believes that President Chavez's commitment to harmony and normalcy is the key challenge and opportunity for Venezuela's future.
