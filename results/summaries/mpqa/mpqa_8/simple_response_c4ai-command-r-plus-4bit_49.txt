SUMMARY: In a show of support for Venezuela's democratic process, former US President Jimmy Carter announces his plans to visit Venezuela following a tumultuous week of conflict. President Hugo Chavez has vowed to open dialogue tables and work towards social harmony, renouncing any intentions of seeking revenge on those who opposed him. President Fernando Henrique Cardoso of Brasil echoes this sentiment, emphasizing the importance of reestablishing normalcy and opening communication channels. These actions demonstrate a commitment to peaceful resolution and a rejection of vengeful retaliation, which is essential for the country's stability and the well-being of its citizens.

END OF SUMMARY.
