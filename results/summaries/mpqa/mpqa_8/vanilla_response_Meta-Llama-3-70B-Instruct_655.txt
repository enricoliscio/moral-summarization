SUMMARY: 
Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso stated that the main challenge facing Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez is to reestablish normalcy after last week's conflicts. Cardoso emphasized the importance of opening communication channels and achieving social harmony. Chavez assured Cardoso that he has no intention of seeking revenge against military personnel who supported the failed coup attempt. Former US President Jimmy Carter has expressed support for Chavez's promise to establish dialogue tables and is scheduled to visit Venezuela soon. Cardoso's comments come as Venezuela works to recover from the recent turmoil. The focus is now on rebuilding and promoting stability in the country. 

END OF SUMMARY.
