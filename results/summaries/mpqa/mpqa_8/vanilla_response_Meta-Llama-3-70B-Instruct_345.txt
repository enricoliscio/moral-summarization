SUMMARY:

Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso has stated that the main challenge facing Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez is to reestablish normalcy in the country following last week's conflicts. Cardoso emphasized the importance of opening communication channels and achieving social harmony. Chavez has assured Cardoso that he has no intention of seeking revenge against the military personnel who supported the failed attempt to remove him from office. The former US President Jimmy Carter has expressed support for Chavez's promise to set up dialogue tables and is scheduled to visit Venezuela soon. The focus is now on rebuilding and promoting dialogue in Venezuela.

END OF SUMMARY.
