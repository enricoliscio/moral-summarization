SUMMARY:

Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso has stated that the main challenge facing Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez is to reestablish normalcy in the country following last week's conflicts. Cardoso emphasized the importance of opening communication channels and achieving social harmony. Chavez has assured Cardoso that he has no intention of seeking revenge against military personnel who supported the failed coup attempt to remove him from office. Former US President Jimmy Carter has expressed support for Chavez's promise to establish dialogue tables and is scheduled to visit Venezuela soon. The focus is on promoting peace and stability in Venezuela, with international leaders backing Chavez's efforts to restore calm and promote national reconciliation.

END OF SUMMARY.
