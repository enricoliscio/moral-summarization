SUMMARY: President Fernando Henrique Cardoso of Brazil has stated that Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez's main challenge is to restore normalcy and social harmony in Venezuela following recent conflicts. Chavez has promised to open communication channels and forego revenge against those who opposed him. Former US President Jimmy Carter has voiced his support for Chavez's efforts and plans to visit Venezuela soon. Cardoso believes that Chavez's focus should be on achieving peace and reconciliation. Chavez's commitment to dialogue and reconciliation is seen as a positive step towards restoring stability in Venezuela.

END OF SUMMARY.
