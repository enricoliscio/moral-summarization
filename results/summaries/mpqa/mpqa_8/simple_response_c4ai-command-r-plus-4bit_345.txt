SUMMARY: Following a tumultuous week of conflict, Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez is faced with the daunting task of restoring peace and normalcy to the nation. In a show of statesmanship, Chavez has vowed to forgo vengeance against those military members who opposed him, instead choosing to focus on reconciliation and dialogue. President Fernando Henrique Cardoso of Brazil emphasizes the importance of open communication and social harmony in achieving this goal. The situation has also garnered international attention and support, with former US President Jimmy Carter offering his endorsement of Chavez's approach and planning a visit to Venezuela. Chavez's commitment to peaceful resolution and his efforts to rebuild unity and stability in Venezuela are being recognized and supported by leaders across the region.
