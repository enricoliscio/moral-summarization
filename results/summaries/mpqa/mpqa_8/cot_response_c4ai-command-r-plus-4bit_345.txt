SUMMARY:
President Fernando Henrique Cardoso of Brazil believes that Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez's main challenge is to restore normalcy after the recent conflicts. Cardoso emphasizes the importance of open communication and social harmony. Notably, Chavez has stated that he will not seek revenge against those in the military who opposed him. This stance is supported by former US President Jimmy Carter, who applauds Chavez's promise to foster dialogue and plans to visit Venezuela soon. Chavez's commitment to reconciliation and peaceful resolution is seen as a positive step towards restoring stability and harmony in Venezuela.
