SUMMARY:

Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso has expressed his support for Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez, emphasizing the need to reestablish normalcy in the country following last week's conflicts. Cardoso stressed the importance of opening communication channels and achieving social harmony. Chavez has assured Cardoso that he will not seek revenge against military personnel who supported the failed coup attempt. The Venezuelan president has also pledged to set up dialogue tables, a move endorsed by former US President Jimmy Carter, who is scheduled to visit Venezuela soon. The focus on reconciliation and dialogue reflects a commitment to peaceful resolution and national unity.

END OF SUMMARY.
