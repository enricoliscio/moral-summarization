SUMMARY: In a statement, Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso says Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez's main challenge is to restore normalcy after recent conflicts. Chavez has promised to open communication channels and seek social harmony, and has no plans to seek revenge on those involved in the coup attempt. Former US President Jimmy Carter supports Chavez's efforts and plans to visit Venezuela soon. Cardoso emphasizes the importance of dialogue and harmony in Venezuela's political and social landscape.
END OF SUMMARY.
