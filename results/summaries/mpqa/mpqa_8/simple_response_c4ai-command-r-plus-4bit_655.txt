SUMMARY: In a show of support for Venezuela's democratic process, former US President Jimmy Carter plans to visit the country following President Hugo Chavez's pledge to foster dialogue and reconciliation in the wake of a failed coup attempt. President Fernando Henrique Cardoso of Brazil emphasizes the critical challenge for Chavez is to restore normalcy and social harmony in Venezuela. Cardoso commends Chavez's commitment to refraining from retaliating against the military involved in the coup, instead prioritizing open communication and peaceful resolution. This development underscores Chavez's dedication to steering Venezuela toward stability and unity, a direction supported by democratic leaders across the region. 

END OF SUMMARY.
