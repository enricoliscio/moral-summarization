SUMMARY: In the wake of recent conflicts in Venezuela, President Hugo Chavez is faced with the daunting task of restoring normalcy and social harmony in the country. President Fernando Henrique Cardoso emphasized the importance of open communication channels to achieve harmony and move forward from the recent unrest. Chavez has assured that he will not seek revenge against those involved in the failed attempt to remove him from power, demonstrating his commitment to reconciliation. The support of former US President Jimmy Carter, who endorsed Chavez's promise to foster dialogue, further underscores the importance of peace and harmony in Venezuela's path forward. Carter's planned visit to Venezuela signals his support for the country's return to stability and social harmony.
END OF SUMMARY.
