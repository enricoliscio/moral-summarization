SUMMARY: In a show of support for Venezuela's President Hugo Chavez, President Fernando Henrique Cardoso of Brazil emphasized the importance of restoring harmony and resolving the recent conflicts in Venezuela. President Chavez has pledged to avoid seeking revenge against those involved in the coup attempt, instead focusing on opening communication channels and fostering social harmony. This commitment to dialogue and reconciliation has been praised by former U.S. President Jimmy Carter, who plans to visit Venezuela soon. The main challenge for President Chavez is to reestablish normalcy and peace in the country, a task that requires addressing the underlying issues that led to the conflict in the first place.
END OF SUMMARY.
