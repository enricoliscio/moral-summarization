SUMMARY: A new crew has arrived at the International Space Station, consisting of two American astronauts and one Russian cosmonaut. The crew, which includes Yuri Onufrienko, Daniel Bursch, and Carl Walz, will be staying at the space station for a half-year. The new crew brought with them personal items reflecting their hobbies, such as fishing gear and a keyboard. They also brought supplies and equipment, which will be returned to Earth once used. The previous crew, led by American Frank Culbertson, will return to Earth after spending 128 days in space. The new crew is eager to begin their mission and contribute to the ongoing research and maintenance of the space station.
END OF SUMMARY.
