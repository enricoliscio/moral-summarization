SUMMARY: A new crew has arrived at the International Space Station, consisting of two American astronauts and one Russian cosmonaut. They will be staying for a half-year mission, taking over from the previous crew that has been on board since August. The new crew brought with them personal items reflecting their hobbies, such as fishing gear, weaving material, and a keyboard. During their stay, they will be conducting various experiments and maintenance tasks, such as unloading and loading the Italian-built cargo carrier, Raffaello, with supplies and equipment. The previous crew, consisting of American Frank Culbertson and Russians Mikhail Tyurin and Vladimir Dezhurov, will return to Earth after a 128-day mission. END OF SUMMARY.
