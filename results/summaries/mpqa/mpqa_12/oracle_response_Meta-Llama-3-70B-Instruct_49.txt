SUMMARY:

In a remarkable display of ingenuity and determination, two astronauts embarked on a critical repair mission to safeguard the International Space Station's solar wings. Astronauts Linda Godwin and Daniel Tani bravely ventured out into the harsh environment of space, utilizing the space shuttle Endeavour's robot arm to reach the faulty motors. Their heroic efforts aimed to shield the motors from the extreme temperatures of space, thereby eliminating power spikes and stalls that could have disastrous consequences. By wrapping thermal covers around the motors, the astronauts ensured the solar wings could continue to generate electricity by pointing towards the sun. This daring feat not only showcases the astronauts' exceptional skill but also underscores the importance of perseverance and quick thinking in the face of adversity.

END OF SUMMARY.
