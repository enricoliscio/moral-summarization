SUMMARY:

In a remarkable display of human ingenuity and determination, two astronauts, Linda Godwin and Daniel Tani, embarked on a critical repair mission to the International Space Station. Their bravery and skill were on full display as they ventured out into the harsh environment of space to wrap thermal covers around the motors that control the station's solar wings. This crucial task aims to protect the motors from extreme temperatures, thereby preventing power spikes and stalls that could jeopardize the station's ability to generate electricity. By successfully completing this mission, the astronauts have ensured the continued functionality of the space station, underscoring the importance of human exploration and innovation in the pursuit of scientific progress. Their selfless efforts will have a lasting impact on our understanding of the universe and our place within it.

END OF SUMMARY.
