SUMMARY: Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso praised the United States for taking positive action on greenhouse gas emissions by adopting measures to control them, despite not formally ratifying the Kyoto Protocol. Cardoso's remarks reflect a moral stance on the issue, acknowledging and welcoming the adoption of environmentally responsible measures by the US, a significant global player in the fight against climate change.

Cardoso expressed hope that the domestic measures taken by the US would have a comparable impact to that of committing to the Kyoto Protocol, demonstrating a pragmatic and optimistic approach to international environmental cooperation.

END OF SUMMARY.
