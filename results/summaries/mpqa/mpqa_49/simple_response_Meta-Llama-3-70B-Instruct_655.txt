SUMMARY:

Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso has expressed optimism about the US decision to adopt measures to control greenhouse gases, despite not ratifying the Kyoto Protocol. Cardoso views the US as a crucial party to the convention and hopes that the domestic measures will have a similar impact to adhering to the Kyoto Protocol. This decision is seen as a positive step towards mitigating the effects of climate change.

Cardoso's stance highlights the importance of international cooperation in addressing global environmental issues. His optimism also underscores the moral imperative of taking action to protect the planet, even if it falls short of ideal agreements. By acknowledging the US decision as a positive step, Cardoso emphasizes the need for collective responsibility and encourages other nations to follow suit.

END OF SUMMARY.
