SUMMARY: Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso has praised the United States' decision to take action on greenhouse gas emissions, despite their formal rejection of the Kyoto Protocol. Cardoso, speaking in Brasilia, expressed hope that domestic measures taken by the US will have a positive impact on the environment, equivalent to that of adhering to the international agreement. This statement highlights a moral commitment to environmental protection and a belief in the potential for countries to take individual responsibility for their emissions, even outside of a formal treaty. Cardoso's comments also acknowledge the significant role and influence of the US in global efforts to address climate change, demonstrating a diplomatic and morally considerate approach to a complex issue.

END OF SUMMARY.
