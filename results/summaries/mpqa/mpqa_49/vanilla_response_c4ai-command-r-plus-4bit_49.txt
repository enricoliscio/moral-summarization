SUMMARY: President Fernando Henrique Cardoso of Brazil praised the US decision to adopt measures to control greenhouse gases, despite their decision not to ratify the Kyoto Protocol. Cardoso acknowledged the importance of the US's role in combating climate change and expressed hope that their domestic efforts would have a similar impact to committing to the protocol. He highlighted the significance of the US's actions given their position as a major contributor to global greenhouse gas emissions. Cardoso's comments come as a response to the US's recent announcement, acknowledging the potential for a positive environmental impact.
