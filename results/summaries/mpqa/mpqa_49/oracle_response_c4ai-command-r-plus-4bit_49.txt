SUMMARY: President Fernando Henrique Cardoso of Brasilia has praised the US for its recent decision to adopt measures to control greenhouse gases, calling it a positive step forward, despite the formal decision not to ratify the Kyoto Protocol. Cardoso's statement emphasizes the importance of the United States' role in combating climate change and expresses hope that the domestically adopted measures will have a similar impact to committing to the Kyoto Protocol. This news highlights a positive development in the global effort to address climate change, demonstrating a commitment to adopting measures that will hopefully have a significant impact.
