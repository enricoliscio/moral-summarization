SUMMARY: President Fernando Henrique Cardoso of Brasilia has declared that the US decision to adopt measures to control greenhouse gases is a positive step forward, despite their formal decision not to ratify the Kyoto Protocol. Cardoso acknowledges the importance of the United States' participation in addressing climate change and expresses hope that the domestic measures taken by the US will have a similar effect to committing to the Protocol. This news highlights a positive development in the global effort to combat climate change, demonstrating a responsible and proactive approach by the US, even without formal adoption of the Protocol.
END OF SUMMARY.
