SUMMARY: Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso responded positively to the US decision to adopt measures to control greenhouse gases, despite their decision not to ratify the Kyoto Protocol. Cardoso acknowledged the importance of the US's role in addressing climate change and expressed hope that their domestic measures would have a similar impact to the protocol. He emphasized the significance of the US's actions given their position as a major player in the global effort to reduce greenhouse gases. Cardoso's statement reflects a diplomatic approach, acknowledging the progress while also alluding to the importance of formal agreements like the Kyoto Protocol in combating climate change on a global scale.
END OF SUMMARY.
