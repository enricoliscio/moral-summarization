SUMMARY:

Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso has welcomed the US decision to take domestic measures to control greenhouse gases, despite not ratifying the Kyoto Protocol. Cardoso views this move as a positive step, emphasizing the significance of the US as a key party to the convention. He hopes that these domestic measures will have a similar impact to adhering to the Kyoto Protocol. This stance reflects Cardoso's commitment to environmental responsibility and cooperation in addressing global climate concerns.

END OF SUMMARY.
