SUMMARY: Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso commented on the US decision to adopt measures to control greenhouse gases, calling it a positive step. Despite the US not formally ratifying the Kyoto Protocol, Cardoso expressed hope that domestic measures taken by the US will have a similar impact on reducing greenhouse gas emissions. He emphasized the importance of the US's role in combating climate change and expressed optimism that their actions will contribute positively to global efforts.
END OF SUMMARY.
