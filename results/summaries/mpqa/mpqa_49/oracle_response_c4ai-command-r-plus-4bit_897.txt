SUMMARY: President Fernando Henrique Cardoso of Brasilia has publicly expressed his approval of the United States' recent decision to take action and adopt measures to control greenhouse gases. Despite the US's formal stance of not ratifying the Kyoto Protocol, Cardoso recognizes the positive nature of their domestic efforts. He highlights the importance of the US as a key player in the fight against climate change and expresses hope that their adopted measures will have a significant impact, equivalent to that of adhering to the Kyoto Protocol. Cardoso's statement acknowledges the potential for diverse paths towards a common goal and encourages a unified front in addressing the global challenge of environmental conservation.
END OF SUMMARY.
