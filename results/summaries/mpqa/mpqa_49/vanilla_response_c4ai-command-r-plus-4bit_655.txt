SUMMARY: Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso praised the US's decision to adopt measures to control greenhouse gases, despite their formal decision to not ratify the Kyoto Protocol. Cardoso highlighted the importance of the US's participation in global efforts to address climate change and expressed hope that their domestic measures would have a similar impact to committing to the protocol. He emphasized the significance of the US's role in combating climate change and anticipated that their actions would contribute positively to international efforts.
