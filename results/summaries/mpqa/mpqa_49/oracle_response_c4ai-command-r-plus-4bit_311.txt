SUMMARY: President Fernando Henrique Cardoso of Brasilia has welcomed the US's recent decision to take measures to control greenhouse gases, despite their previous rejection of the Kyoto Protocol. Cardoso recognizes the significance of the US's involvement in addressing climate change, referring to their decision to adopt measures as a "positive" step forward. He expresses hope that the domestic measures taken by the US will have a comparable impact to that of committing to the Kyoto Protocol, acknowledging their crucial role in combating global environmental issues. This news highlights a positive development in the international effort to mitigate climate change, with Cardoso's response emphasizing the potential benefits of the US's actions.
