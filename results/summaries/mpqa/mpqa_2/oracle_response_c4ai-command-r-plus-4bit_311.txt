SUMMARY: China has disapproved of the use of the phrase "axis of evil" in international relations, with Foreign Ministry spokesman Kong Quan stating that such wording could have serious consequences. Kong emphasized that China believes anti-terrorism campaigns should be based on solid evidence and should not be arbitrarily expanded. He further stressed the importance of adhering to the principles of the United Nations Charter, the Five Principles of Peaceful Co-existence, and the equal treatment of all countries in handling state-to-state relations. China's position upholds the safeguarding of long-term peace and stability in the world and affected regions. China's stance demonstrates a commitment to moral principles and a thoughtful approach to international relations, prioritizing peace and equality.

END OF SUMMARY.
