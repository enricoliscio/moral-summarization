SUMMARY: China disapproves of the use of the term "axis of evil" to describe nations, as stated by Foreign Ministry spokesman Kong Quan. Kong expressed concern that the use of such inflammatory language could have serious consequences and is not conducive to maintaining peace and stability in the world. He emphasized that China advocates for a more cautious and respectful approach to international relations, based on irrefutable evidence, adherence to the UN Charter and the principles of equal treatment for all nations. China's stance highlights the importance of diplomacy and a measured response in the global arena, especially in the context of anti-terrorism efforts. This statement serves as a reminder that words matter and can have a significant impact on international relations and global stability. END OF SUMMARY.
