SUMMARY: China has disapproved of the use of the term "axis of evil" in international relations, with Foreign Ministry spokesman Kong Quan stating that such wording could have serious consequences. Kong emphasized that China always upholds the principles of irrefutable evidence and equal treatment of all countries in anti-terrorism efforts and believes that following these principles, as well as the UN Charter and the Five Principles of Peaceful Co-existence, is the only way to safeguard long-term peace and stability in the world. China's stance on this matter highlights its commitment to a peaceful and equitable approach to international relations and its disapproval of arbitrary expansions of anti-terrorism campaigns.

END OF SUMMARY.
