SUMMARY: China voices strong disapproval of the use of the incendiary term "axis of evil" to describe nations deemed hostile, urging caution and respect for international norms in the conduct of international relations.

Spokesman Kong Quan expresses concern over the inflammatory label, emphasizing the importance of evidence-based decision-making and caution in anti-terrorism efforts. He stresses that relations between nations must adhere to the principles of the UN Charter, peaceful coexistence, and equal treatment of all countries to maintain global peace and stability. China recognizes the disagreement within U.S. public opinion regarding the "axis of evil" label and foresees serious consequences if such divisive rhetoric guides policy.

China's stance reflects a moral commitment to diplomacy, peace, and respect for international norms, urging a more nuanced and evidence-based approach to global affairs.

END OF SUMMARY.
