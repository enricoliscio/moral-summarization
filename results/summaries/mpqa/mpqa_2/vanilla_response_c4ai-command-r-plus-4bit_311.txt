SUMMARY: China's Foreign Ministry spokesman Kong Quan expressed disapproval of the use of the term "axis of evil" in international relations. He stated that China does not agree with the wording and believes that the consequences could be severe if such logic is followed. Kong emphasized that anti-terrorism campaigns should be based on solid evidence and should not be arbitrarily expanded. He also highlighted the importance of adhering to the principles of the UN Charter, the Five Principles of Peaceful Co-existence, and equal treatment of all countries in handling state-to-state relations. China's stance aims to promote long-term peace and stability in the world and affected regions. China has noticed the disagreement within U.S. public opinion regarding the use of the term and believes that the use of such provocative language could have detrimental effects on international relations and stability.
END OF SUMMARY.
