SUMMARY: At the first Asia-Europe Meeting Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing, Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized his country's commitment to environmental protection and improvement. Minister Nha highlighted Vietnam's efforts in addressing environmental issues such as pollution prevention, biodiversity protection, and enhancing the environment in industrial, urban, and rural areas. He also stressed the significance of bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe in tackling these issues. The minister proposed the establishment of a biennial cooperation forum on the environment within the Asia-Europe Environment Cooperation Program to facilitate dialogue and the exchange of information and expertise. The meeting concluded with a call for the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and enhanced cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members. Vietnam's stance demonstrates its values and commitment to protecting the environment and promoting international cooperation in dealing with global environmental challenges. END OF SUMMARY.
