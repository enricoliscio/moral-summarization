SUMMARY: At the first Asia-Europe Meeting (ASEM) Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing, Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology, and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized his country's commitment to environmental protection. He highlighted Vietnam's efforts in addressing environmental issues through mobilizing resources and international cooperation. The minister proposed a cooperation forum on the environment to be held biennially within the Asia-Europe Environment Cooperation Program to facilitate discussions and exchanges on environmental policies and information. The ASEM Environment Ministers' Meeting concluded with a call for the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and enhanced cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members. The meeting underscored the importance of bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe in tackling environmental challenges.

END OF SUMMARY.
