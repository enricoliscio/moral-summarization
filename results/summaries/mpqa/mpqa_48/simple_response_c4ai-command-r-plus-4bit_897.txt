SUMMARY: At the first Asia-Europe Meeting Environment Ministers' Meeting, Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology, and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized his country's commitment to environmental protection and sustainable development. He highlighted Vietnam's efforts to mobilize resources and enhance international cooperation to address environmental challenges. Minister Nha also proposed the establishment of a biennial cooperation forum on the environment within the Asia-Europe Environment Cooperation Program to facilitate dialogue and the exchange of information and expertise. The ASEM Environment Ministers' Meeting concluded with a call for the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and enhanced cooperation and dialogue between Asia and Europe on environmental issues, reflecting a shared responsibility and moral obligation to protect our planet.

END OF SUMMARY.
