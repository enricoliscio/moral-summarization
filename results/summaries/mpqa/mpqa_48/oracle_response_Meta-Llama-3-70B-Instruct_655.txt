SUMMARY: Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology and Environment Chu Tuan Nha emphasized the importance of environmental protection and values the prevention of pollution and degradation, bio-diversity protection, and improvement of the environment in industrial zones and urban and rural areas. The Vietnamese Government has mobilized all resources and strengthened international cooperation to address environmental issues. Nha stressed the significance of bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe in dealing with environmental issues, highlighting the need for a cooperation forum on the environment to facilitate dialogue and information exchange. The ASEM Environment Ministers' Meeting concluded with a call for early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and cooperation among ASEM members. The meeting served as a foundation for future cooperation between the two continents, underscoring the importance of addressing environmental issues through collective effort. END OF SUMMARY.
