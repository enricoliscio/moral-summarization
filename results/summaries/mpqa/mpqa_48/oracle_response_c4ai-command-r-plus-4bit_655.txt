SUMMARY: Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized his country's commitment to environmental protection and improvement at the first Asia-Europe Meeting Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing. Minister Nha highlighted Vietnam's efforts in addressing environmental issues, including pollution prevention, biodiversity protection, and enhancing the environment in industrial, urban, and rural areas. He advocated for increased bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe, suggesting a biennial cooperation forum on the environment to facilitate dialogue and the exchange of information and expertise. The meeting concluded with a call for the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and enhanced cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members. Minister Nha's statements and the meeting's outcome demonstrate a shared commitment to dealing with environmental challenges and promoting sustainable practices.

END OF SUMMARY.
