SUMMARY:
Vietnam's Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized the importance of environmental protection and cooperation between Asia and Europe at the first Asia-Europe Meeting Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing. He stressed that Vietnam values environmental protection, including prevention of pollution and degradation, bio-diversity protection, and improvement of the environment in industrial zones and urban and rural areas. The Vietnamese Government has mobilized all resources and strengthened international cooperation to address environmental issues. Nha called for the establishment of a cooperation forum on the environment, which will facilitate the exchange of dialogues on policy and information, and experts on the environment. The meeting concluded with the adoption of a Statement of the Presidium, calling for an early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and cooperation and dialogue between ASEM members. END OF SUMMARY.
