SUMMARY:

Vietnam's Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized the country's commitment to environmental protection at the first Asia-Europe Meeting Environment Ministers' Meeting (ASEM EnMM) in Beijing. He highlighted the importance of bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe in addressing environmental issues and proposed the establishment of a cooperation forum on the environment, to be held every two years. The forum aims to facilitate policy dialogue, information exchange, and expert collaboration on environmental issues. The ASEM EnMM concluded with the adoption of a statement calling for the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and cooperation among ASEM members. Vietnam has mobilized all resources and strengthened international cooperation to address environmental issues, including pollution prevention, biodiversity protection, and environmental improvement in industrial zones and urban and rural areas.

END OF SUMMARY.
