SUMMARY: At the first Asia-Europe Meeting (ASEM) Environment Ministers' Meeting (ASEM EnMM) in Beijing, Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized his country's commitment to environmental protection and improvement. Minister Nha highlighted Vietnam's efforts in addressing environmental issues, including pollution prevention, biodiversity protection, and enhancing the environment in industrial, urban, and rural areas. He advocated for the mobilization of resources and international cooperation to tackle these issues effectively. Minister Nha proposed the establishment of a biennial cooperation forum on the environment within the Asia-Europe Environment Cooperation Program to facilitate dialogue and the exchange of information and expertise. The ASEM EnMM concluded with a call for the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and enhanced cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members in dealing with environmental challenges. Vietnam's stance demonstrates its values and commitment to protecting the environment and promoting sustainable practices. END OF SUMMARY.
