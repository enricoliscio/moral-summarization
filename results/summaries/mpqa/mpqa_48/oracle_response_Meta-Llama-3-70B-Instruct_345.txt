SUMMARY: Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology and Environment Chu Tuan Nha emphasized the importance of environmental protection, including prevention of pollution and degradation, bio-diversity protection, and improvement of the environment in industrial zones and urban and rural areas. The Vietnamese Government has mobilized all resources and strengthened international cooperation to address environmental issues. Nha stressed the significance of bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe in dealing with environmental issues, calling for the establishment of a cooperation forum on the environment to facilitate the exchange of policy dialogues and expert information. The ASEM Environment Ministers' Meeting concluded with a Statement of the Presidium urging the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and promoting cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members. By valuing environmental protection, Vietnam is taking a crucial step towards a more sustainable future. END OF SUMMARY.
