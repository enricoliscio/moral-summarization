SUMMARY:

Vietnam's Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized the country's commitment to environmental protection at the Asia-Europe Meeting (ASEM) Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing. Nha stressed the importance of preventing pollution and degradation, protecting biodiversity, and improving the environment in industrial zones and urban and rural areas. The Vietnamese government has mobilized all resources and strengthened international cooperation to address environmental issues. Nha highlighted the significance of bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe in dealing with environmental issues and proposed the establishment of a cooperation forum on the environment, which will facilitate the exchange of policy dialogues and expert information. The meeting concluded with a call for early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and cooperation among ASEM members.

END OF SUMMARY.
