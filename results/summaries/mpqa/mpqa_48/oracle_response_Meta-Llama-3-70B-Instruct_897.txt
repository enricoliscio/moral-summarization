SUMMARY: Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology and Environment Chu Tuan Nha emphasized the importance of environmental protection and values the prevention of pollution and degradation. He stressed that the Vietnamese Government is committed to addressing environmental issues and has mobilized all resources to improve the environment in industrial zones, urban, and rural areas. Nha highlighted the significance of bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe in dealing with environmental issues, calling for the establishment of a cooperation forum on the environment to facilitate the exchange of policy dialogues and expert information. The ASEM Environment Ministers' Meeting concluded with a statement urging the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and promoting cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members. By working together, Asia and Europe can effectively address environmental challenges and protect biodiversity. END OF SUMMARY.
