SUMMARY: At the first Asia-Europe Meeting on the environment, Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology, and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized his country's commitment to environmental protection and sustainable development. He highlighted Vietnam's efforts to mobilize resources and strengthen international cooperation to address pressing environmental challenges. The minister also proposed the establishment of a biennial cooperation forum on the environment to facilitate dialogue, policy exchange, and the sharing of expertise between Asia and Europe. The meeting concluded with a call for the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and enhanced cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members. This demonstrates a shared moral responsibility and a commitment to global environmental stewardship and sustainability.

END OF SUMMARY.
