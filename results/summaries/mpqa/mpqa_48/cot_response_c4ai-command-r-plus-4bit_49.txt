SUMMARY: Vietnam's Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized his country's strong commitment to environmental protection and cooperation between Asia and Europe during the first ASEM Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing. Minister Nha highlighted Vietnam's efforts in preventing pollution, protecting biodiversity, and improving the environment in industrial, urban, and rural areas. He stressed the importance of bilateral cooperation and dialogue between the two continents, proposing a biennial cooperation forum on the environment to facilitate the exchange of policies, information, and expertise. The ASEM EnMM concluded with a call for the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and enhanced cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members, reflecting a shared commitment to addressing environmental challenges through international cooperation and collective action.
