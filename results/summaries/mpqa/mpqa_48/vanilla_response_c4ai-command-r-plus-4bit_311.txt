SUMMARY: At the first Asia-Europe Meeting (ASEM) Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing, Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized his country's commitment to environmental protection and international cooperation in addressing environmental issues. He proposed the establishment of a biennial cooperation forum on the environment within the Asia-Europe Environment Cooperation Program to facilitate dialogue and exchange of information and experts. The ASEM EnMM concluded with a call for the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and enhanced cooperation and dialogue between Asia and Europe on environmental matters. The meeting highlighted the shared commitment of Asian and European countries to address environmental challenges and promote sustainable development.

END OF SUMMARY.
