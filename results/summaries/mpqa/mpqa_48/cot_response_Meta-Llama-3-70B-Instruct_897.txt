SUMMARY:
Vietnam's Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized the importance of environmental protection at the Asia-Europe Meeting (ASEM) Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing. He highlighted Vietnam's commitment to values such as prevention of pollution and degradation, bio-diversity protection, and improvement of the environment in industrial zones and urban and rural areas. The Minister stressed the importance of bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe in addressing environmental issues, calling for the establishment of a cooperation forum on the environment to facilitate dialogue and exchange of information and experts. The meeting concluded with the adoption of a Statement of the Presidium, urging for an early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and cooperation and dialogue between ASEM members. END OF SUMMARY.
