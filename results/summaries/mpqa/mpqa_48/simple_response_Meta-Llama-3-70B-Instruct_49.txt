SUMMARY:

Vietnam's Minister of Science, Technology, and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized the country's commitment to environmental protection at the Asia-Europe Meeting Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing. He highlighted the importance of preventing pollution, protecting biodiversity, and improving the environment in industrial zones and urban and rural areas. Nha stressed the need for bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe to address environmental issues, suggesting the establishment of a cooperation forum on the environment to facilitate dialogue and information exchange. The meeting concluded with a call to implement the Kyoto Protocol and promote cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members. The moral framing of this news article emphasizes the importance of international cooperation and collective responsibility in protecting the environment and mitigating the effects of climate change.

END OF SUMMARY.
