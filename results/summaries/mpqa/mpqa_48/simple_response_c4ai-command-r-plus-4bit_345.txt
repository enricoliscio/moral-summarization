SUMMARY: At the first Asia-Europe Meeting Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing, Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology, and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized his country's commitment to environmental protection and sustainable development. He highlighted Vietnam's efforts to mobilize resources and enhance international cooperation to address pressing environmental challenges. Minister Nha also proposed the establishment of a biennial cooperation forum on the environment within the Asia-Europe Environment Cooperation Program to foster dialogue and the exchange of expertise and policies. His remarks reflected Vietnam's responsible and proactive approach to environmental issues, demonstrating the country's dedication to playing a constructive role in global environmental governance. The meeting concluded with a call for the prompt implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and enhanced cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members, signifying a united front in tackling environmental concerns. 

END OF SUMMARY.
