SUMMARY:
The first Asia-Europe Meeting Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing highlighted Vietnam's commitment to environmental protection and improvement. Vietnam's Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, stressed the country's efforts in addressing pollution, biodiversity protection, and enhancing the environment in industrial, urban, and rural areas. He emphasized the value Vietnam places on environmental protection and its dedication to bilateral cooperation with other nations. The minister proposed a biennial cooperation forum within the Asia-Europe Environment Cooperation Program to facilitate dialogue and exchange of expertise and policies. The ASEM EnMM concluded with a call to action, urging the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and fostering cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members. This meeting laid the foundation for future Asia-Europe environmental cooperation, with a shared emphasis on the importance of addressing global environmental challenges through collaboration and mutual improvement.
