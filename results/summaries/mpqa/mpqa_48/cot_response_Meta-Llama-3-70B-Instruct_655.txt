SUMMARY:
Vietnam's Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized the importance of environmental protection and cooperation at the Asia-Europe Meeting Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing. He stressed that Vietnam values environmental protection, including prevention of pollution and degradation, and bio-diversity protection. The Vietnamese Government has mobilized all resources and strengthened international cooperation to address environmental issues. Nha called for bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe to deal with environmental issues, highlighting the importance of dialogue and cooperation. He proposed the establishment of a cooperation forum on the environment to facilitate the exchange of policy and information. The meeting concluded with the adoption of a Statement of the Presidium, calling for the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and cooperation between ASEM members.
