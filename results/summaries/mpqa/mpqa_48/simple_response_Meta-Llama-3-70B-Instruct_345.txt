SUMMARY:

Vietnam's Minister of Science, Technology, and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized the importance of environmental protection at the Asia-Europe Meeting Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing. He highlighted Vietnam's commitment to preventing pollution, protecting biodiversity, and improving the environment in industrial zones and urban and rural areas. The minister stressed the need for bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe to address environmental issues and proposed the establishment of a cooperation forum on the environment, which would facilitate the exchange of policy dialogues and expert information. The meeting concluded with the adoption of a statement calling for the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and cooperation among ASEM members. The moral framing of this article emphasizes the importance of international cooperation and collective responsibility in protecting the environment and addressing environmental issues.

END OF SUMMARY.
