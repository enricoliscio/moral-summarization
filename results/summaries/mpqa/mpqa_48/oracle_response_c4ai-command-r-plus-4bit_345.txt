SUMMARY: At the first Asia-Europe Meeting Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing, Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology, and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized his country's commitment to environmental values and protection. He highlighted Vietnam's efforts in addressing critical issues such as pollution prevention, biodiversity protection, and environmental improvement in industrial, urban, and rural areas. The minister also acknowledged the importance of bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe in dealing with transnational environmental challenges. He proposed the establishment of a biennial cooperation forum on the environment within the Asia-Europe Environment Cooperation Program to facilitate dialogue and the exchange of expertise and information. The meeting concluded with a call for the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and enhanced cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members. This demonstrates a shared commitment to addressing global environmental concerns and promoting sustainable practices. 

END OF SUMMARY.
