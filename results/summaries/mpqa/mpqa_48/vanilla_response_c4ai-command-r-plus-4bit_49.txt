SUMMARY: At the first Asia-Europe Meeting (ASEM) Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing, Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology, and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized his country's commitment to environmental protection and international cooperation on these issues. He proposed a biennial cooperation forum on the environment within the Asia-Europe Environment Cooperation Program to facilitate dialogue and exchange of information and experts. The ASEM EnMM concluded with a call for the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and enhanced cooperation and dialogue between Asia and Europe on environmental matters. The Vietnamese Minister's remarks highlighted Vietnam's efforts and the importance of bilateral cooperation between the two continents in addressing global environmental challenges. The meeting also underscored the need for collaborative action and dialogue among ASEM members to tackle these issues effectively.

END OF SUMMARY.
