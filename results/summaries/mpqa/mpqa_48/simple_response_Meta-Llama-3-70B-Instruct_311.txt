SUMMARY: 

Vietnam's Minister of Science, Technology, and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized the importance of environmental protection at the Asia-Europe Meeting Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing. He highlighted Vietnam's commitment to preventing pollution, protecting biodiversity, and improving the environment in industrial zones and urban and rural areas. The minister stressed the need for bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe to address environmental issues and proposed the establishment of a cooperation forum on the environment to facilitate dialogue and information exchange. The meeting concluded with a call to implement the Kyoto Protocol and promote cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members. The moral framing of this news article emphasizes the importance of collective responsibility and cooperation in protecting the environment, recognizing that environmental issues are a shared concern that requires joint efforts to address.

END OF SUMMARY.
