SUMMARY: Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology and Environment Chu Tuan Nha emphasized the importance of environmental protection and values the prevention of pollution and degradation, bio-diversity protection, and improvement of the environment in industrial zones and urban and rural areas. The Vietnamese Government has mobilized all resources and strengthened its international cooperation in addressing environmental issues. Nha stressed the significance of bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe in dealing with environmental issues, calling for the establishment of a cooperation forum on the environment to facilitate the exchange of dialogues on policy and information and experts on the environment. The ASEM Environment Ministers' Meeting concluded with the adoption of a Statement of the Presidium, urging an early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and cooperation and dialogue between ASEM members. END OF SUMMARY.
