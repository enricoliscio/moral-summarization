SUMMARY:
The Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized the importance of bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe in addressing environmental issues. He conveyed Vietnam's commitment to environmental protection, including the prevention of pollution and biodiversity protection, and the improvement of industrial, urban, and rural areas. Minister Nha highlighted Vietnam's efforts in mobilizing resources and strengthening international cooperation on these issues. He proposed the establishment of a biennial cooperation forum on the environment within the Asia-Europe Environment Cooperation Program to facilitate policy dialogues, information exchanges, and expert collaborations. The first ASEM Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing concluded with a call for the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and enhanced cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members. The meeting underscored the shared commitment to environmental protection and the value of international cooperation in addressing global environmental challenges.
