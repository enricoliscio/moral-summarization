SUMMARY:
The Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized the country's commitment to environmental protection and improvement during the first Asia-Europe Meeting Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing. Minister Nha highlighted Vietnam's efforts in addressing environmental issues, including the prevention of pollution and the protection of biodiversity. He underscored the importance of bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe in dealing with these issues, suggesting that cooperation and dialogue between the two continents will serve as a foundation for future collaboration. The minister called for the establishment of a biennial cooperation forum on the environment within the Asia-Europe Environment Cooperation Program to facilitate the exchange of policies, information, and experts. The meeting concluded with the adoption of a statement by the presidium, emphasizing the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and promoting cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members.
END OF SUMMARY.
