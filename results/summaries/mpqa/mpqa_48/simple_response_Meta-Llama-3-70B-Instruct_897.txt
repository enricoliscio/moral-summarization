SUMMARY:

Vietnam's Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized the country's commitment to environmental protection at the Asia-Europe Meeting Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing. He highlighted the importance of bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe in addressing environmental issues and proposed the establishment of a cooperation forum on the environment to facilitate dialogue and information exchange. The meeting concluded with a call for early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and cooperation among ASEM members. Nha's message underscored the moral imperative of environmental protection, emphasizing the need for collective action to prevent pollution, protect biodiversity, and improve the environment in industrial zones and urban and rural areas.

END OF SUMMARY.
