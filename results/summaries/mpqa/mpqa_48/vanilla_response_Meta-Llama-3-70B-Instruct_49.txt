SUMMARY:

Vietnam's Minister of Science, Technology, and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized the importance of environmental protection at the Asia-Europe Meeting (ASEM) Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing. He highlighted Vietnam's efforts to prevent pollution, protect biodiversity, and improve the environment in industrial zones and urban and rural areas. The minister stressed the need for bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe to address environmental issues and proposed the establishment of a cooperation forum on the environment to facilitate policy dialogue and expert exchange. The meeting concluded with the adoption of a statement calling for the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and cooperation among ASEM members.

END OF SUMMARY.
