SUMMARY: At the first Asia-Europe Meeting Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing, Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology, and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized his country's commitment to environmental protection and sustainable development. Minister Nha highlighted Vietnam's efforts in addressing environmental issues through resource mobilization and international cooperation. He also proposed the establishment of a biennial cooperation forum on the environment within the Asia-Europe Environment Cooperation Program to facilitate dialogue and exchange of information and expertise. The meeting concluded with the adoption of a statement by the presidium, calling for the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and promoting cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members on environmental matters. The statement also emphasized the importance of bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe in tackling environmental challenges.

END OF SUMMARY.
