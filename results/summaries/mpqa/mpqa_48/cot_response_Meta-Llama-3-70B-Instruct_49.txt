SUMMARY:
At the Asia-Europe Meeting (ASEM) Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing, Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology and Environment Chu Tuan Nha emphasized Vietnam's commitment to environmental protection, including the prevention of pollution and degradation, and biodiversity protection. He stressed the importance of bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe in addressing environmental issues, calling for the establishment of a cooperation forum on the environment to facilitate dialogue and exchange of information and experts. The meeting concluded with the adoption of a Statement of the Presidium, urging an early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and promoting cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members. The minister's remarks highlighted the values of environmental protection and cooperation in addressing global environmental challenges. END OF SUMMARY.
