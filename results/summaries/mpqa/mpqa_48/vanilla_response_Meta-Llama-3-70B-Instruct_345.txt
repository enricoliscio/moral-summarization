SUMMARY:

Vietnam's Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized the country's commitment to environmental protection at the Asia-Europe Meeting (ASEM) Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing. He highlighted the importance of bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe in addressing environmental issues and proposed the establishment of a cooperation forum on the environment to be held every two years. The forum would facilitate the exchange of policy dialogues, information, and expert knowledge on the environment. The meeting concluded with the adoption of a Statement of the Presidium, calling for the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and cooperation and dialogue between ASEM members. Vietnam has mobilized all resources and strengthened international cooperation to address environmental issues, including pollution prevention, biodiversity protection, and environmental improvement in industrial zones and urban and rural areas.

END OF SUMMARY.
