SUMMARY: At the first Asia-Europe Meeting on the environment, Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology, and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized the country's commitment to environmental protection and sustainable development. Minister Nha highlighted Vietnam's efforts to mobilize resources and foster international cooperation to address environmental challenges. He proposed a biennial cooperation forum on the environment to enhance policy dialogue, information exchange, and expert collaboration between Asia and Europe. The meeting concluded with a call for the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and strengthened cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members on environmental issues, reflecting a shared responsibility and global commitment to protecting our planet's health and biodiversity.

END OF SUMMARY.
