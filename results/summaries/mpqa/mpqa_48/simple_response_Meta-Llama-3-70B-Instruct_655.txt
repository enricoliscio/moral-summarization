SUMMARY:

Vietnam's Minister of Science, Technology, and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized the importance of environmental protection at the Asia-Europe Meeting Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing. He highlighted Vietnam's commitment to preventing pollution, protecting biodiversity, and improving the environment in industrial zones and urban and rural areas. Nha stressed the need for bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe to address environmental issues and proposed the establishment of a cooperation forum to facilitate dialogue and information exchange. The meeting concluded with a call to implement the Kyoto Protocol and promote cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members. The moral framing of this article emphasizes the importance of collective action and international cooperation in addressing environmental issues, highlighting the shared responsibility of nations to protect the environment for future generations.

END OF SUMMARY.
