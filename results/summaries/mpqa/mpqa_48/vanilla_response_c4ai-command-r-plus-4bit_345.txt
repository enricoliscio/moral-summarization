SUMMARY: At the first Asia-Europe Meeting (ASEM) Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing, Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, emphasized his country's commitment to environmental protection and international cooperation on these issues. Minister Nha highlighted the importance of bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe and proposed a cooperation forum on the environment to be held every two years within the Asia-Europe Environment Cooperation Program. The forum would facilitate the exchange of dialogue, policies, information, and experts. The ASEM Environment Ministers' Meeting concluded with a call for the early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and enhanced cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members.

END OF SUMMARY.
