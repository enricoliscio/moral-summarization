SUMMARY: Leading Pakistani defense analyst and former ISI chief Hameed Gul rejects the US State Department's recent report on human rights as propaganda. He accuses the US of attempting to subjugate the world and disregarding human rights, norms, and religious values. Hameed Gul highlights the ongoing violence against Muslims in India and Palestine, criticizing the silence of the US and other nations. He asserts that the US only acknowledges human rights violations when they align with its interests. He calls for Muslim scholars and rulers to unite and resist increasing US influence, warning of dire consequences if they remain indifferent. Hameed Gul's statements reflect a strong anti-US sentiment and a belief in the need for Muslim solidarity in the face of perceived oppression.

END OF SUMMARY.
