SUMMARY:

China has released its annual report on human rights in the United States, criticizing Washington for ignoring its own abuses while accusing other countries of similar violations. The report, issued by China's Cabinet, highlights police abuses, poverty, racial discrimination, and lack of personal safety in the US. It also accuses the US of infringing on other nations' sovereignty through military operations and overseas deployments. Additionally, the report criticizes the US for withdrawing from the Kyoto Protocol on reducing greenhouse gases. The report is a response to the US State Department's annual report on global human rights conditions, which typically accuses China of widespread abuses. China's report accuses the US of distorting human rights conditions in other countries, including China, while ignoring its own problems.

END OF SUMMARY.
