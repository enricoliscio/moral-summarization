SUMMARY:

China has released its annual report on human rights in the United States, criticizing Washington for ignoring its own human rights abuses while criticizing other countries. The report lists evidence of police abuses, poverty, racial discrimination, and lack of personal safety in the US. It also accuses the US of infringing on other nations' sovereignty through military operations and stationing troops overseas. Additionally, the report slams the US for withdrawing from the Kyoto Protocol on cutting greenhouse gases. The report is a response to the US State Department's annual report on global human rights conditions, which usually accuses China of widespread abuses. China's report accuses the US of distorting human rights conditions in other countries, including China, while ignoring its own problems.

END OF SUMMARY.
