SUMMARY: In response to the US State Department's annual report on global human rights conditions, China released its own report criticizing the United States for turning a blind eye to its own human rights abuses while criticizing other nations. The Chinese report accused the US of police abuses, poverty, racial discrimination, lack of personal safety, and wantonly infringing on the sovereignty of other nations through military actions. It also criticized the US for withdrawing from the Kyoto Protocol. China's report was released in response to the US report, which accused China of widespread human rights violations, including distorted human rights conditions and accusations of violations.

END OF SUMMARY.
