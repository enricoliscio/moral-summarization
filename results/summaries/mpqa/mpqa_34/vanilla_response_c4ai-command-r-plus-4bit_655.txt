SUMMARY: China released its annual report on human rights in the United States, accusing the US of ignoring its own human rights issues while criticizing other countries. The report highlighted issues such as police abuses, poverty, racial discrimination, and lack of personal safety in the US. It also criticized US military operations and the withdrawal from the Kyoto Protocol. This report is China's response to the US State Department's report on global human rights conditions, which often criticizes China. China's report stated that the US acts as a "world judge of human rights" while ignoring its own human rights problems.
END OF SUMMARY.
