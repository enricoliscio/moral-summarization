SUMMARY: Iranian President Mohammad Khatami congratulated Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez following his return to power after a failed military coup. Khatami expressed support for Chavez's government and their shared goal of oil production cuts to raise prices globally. Khatami and Chavez discussed the recent events in Venezuela, with Chavez attributing the coup to those who wanted Venezuela to leave OPEC. Chavez expressed gratitude for Khatami's support and the two presidents planned to meet in the future. Iran had previously sent congratulations to Venezuela for the reinstatement of Chavez's government and condemned the coup, emphasizing the importance of democratic processes. Chavez, a supporter of OPEC and oil production cuts, resumed his duties as president after being detained during the coup attempt.

END OF SUMMARY.
