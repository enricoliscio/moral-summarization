SUMMARY: Iranian President Mohammad Khatami congratulated Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez on his return to power after a failed military coup, calling it a victory for justice and freedom. Khatami expressed his support for Chavez's government and its objectives, while Chavez attributed the failure of the coup to the support of the Venezuelan people and God. Chavez also revealed the coup leaders' intention to withdraw Venezuela from OPEC, citing their desire to increase oil production and undermine the organization. This event highlights the shared values of justice, freedom, and democratic legitimacy between the two leaders and their nations. Khatami's congratulations and Iran's previous support for Venezuela's return to the rule of law demonstrate a commitment to these moral principles on an international scale. END OF SUMMARY.
