SUMMARY: President Mohammad Khatami of Iran congratulated Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez on his return to power after a failed military coup. Khatami praised the restoration of the legitimate and democratically elected government as a victory of justice and freedom. He expressed hope that the Venezuelan government would now achieve its exalted objectives with popular support. President Chavez described the coup attempt as anti-democratic and thanked President Khatami for his support. He also explained the objective of the coup plotters was to have Venezuela leave OPEC and increase oil production. Iran had previously congratulated Venezuela on the reinstatement of the rule of law and the legitimate government of President Chavez, condemning the coup and the use of force as an unacceptable method for political change in modern human society. Chavez, a staunch supporter of oil production cuts, was detained for two days but resumed his functions as president with the support of the people and the Almighty. Khatami and Chavez expressed a mutual desire to meet in the near future.
END OF SUMMARY.
