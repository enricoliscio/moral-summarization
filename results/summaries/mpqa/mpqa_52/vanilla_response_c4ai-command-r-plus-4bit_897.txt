SUMMARY: Iranian President Mohammad Khatami congratulated Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez following his return to power after a failed military coup. Khatami praised the victory of justice and freedom and expressed hope for Venezuela to achieve its objectives. Chavez shared that the coup attempt aimed to remove Venezuela from OPEC, and both presidents expressed a desire to meet. Iran had previously sent congratulations and condemned the coup, supporting the reinstatement of the democratically elected Chavez, who is a supporter of oil production cuts to boost prices. Khatami and Chavez's conversation highlighted the mutual respect and desire for cooperation between the two nations.

END OF SUMMARY.
