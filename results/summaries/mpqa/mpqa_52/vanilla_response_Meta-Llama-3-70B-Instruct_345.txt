SUMMARY:

Iranian President Mohammad Khatami congratulated Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez on his return to power after a failed military coup. In a phone call, Khatami praised the Venezuelan nation for their victory and restoration of the legitimate government, calling it a triumph of justice and freedom. Chavez briefed Khatami on the latest developments in Venezuela, stating that the coup plotters aimed to remove Venezuela from OPEC and increase oil production to undermine the organization. Chavez thanked Khatami for his support and the two leaders expressed a desire to meet soon. Iran had earlier condemned the coup, calling it an unacceptable method for political change. Chavez, a strong supporter of oil production cuts, was reinstated as president after being detained on a Caribbean island.

END OF SUMMARY.
