SUMMARY:

Iranian President Mohammad Khatami congratulated Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez on his return to power after a failed military coup. In a phone call, Khatami praised the Venezuelan nation's victory and restoration of the legitimate government, calling it a triumph of justice and freedom. Chavez briefed Khatami on the latest developments in Venezuela and expressed gratitude for Iran's support. Chavez also revealed that the coup plotters aimed to remove Venezuela from OPEC and increase oil production to undermine the organization. The two leaders expressed a desire to meet soon. Earlier, Iran had condemned the coup and welcomed Chavez's return, praising his commitment to oil production cuts to boost prices.

END OF SUMMARY.
