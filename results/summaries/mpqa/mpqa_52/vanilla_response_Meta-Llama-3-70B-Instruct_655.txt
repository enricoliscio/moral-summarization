SUMMARY:

Iranian President Mohammad Khatami congratulated Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez on his return to power after a failed military coup. During a phone call, Khatami hailed Chavez's return as a "victory of justice and freedom" and expressed hope that the Venezuelan government would achieve its objectives with the people's support. Chavez briefed Khatami on the latest developments in his country and attributed his return to power to the help of God and the people. He also revealed that the coup plotters aimed to remove Venezuela from OPEC and increase oil production to undermine the organization. The two presidents expressed a desire to meet soon. Iran had earlier condemned the coup and welcomed Chavez's reinstatement, praising his commitment to oil production cuts to boost prices.

END OF SUMMARY.
