SUMMARY: Brazil and Germany have issued a joint document, "Partnership Brasil-Germany: Action Plan", calling for the implementation of the Kyoto Protocol by the Sustainable Development Summit (Rio+10) in September. The document urges other countries to ratify and enforce the protocol. Both nations plan to expand cooperation on environmental and sustainable development issues, presenting common positions at the Rio+10 meeting. The document also highlights the importance of the Pilot Program for Protection of Tropical Forests (PPG-7) and Germany's participation in the program. The joint effort aims to promote sustainable development and environmental protection globally. By working together, Brazil and Germany hope to make a significant impact at the upcoming summit. END OF SUMMARY.
