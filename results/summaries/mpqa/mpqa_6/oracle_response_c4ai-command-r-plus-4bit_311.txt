SUMMARY: Brazil and Germany are leading the way in the fight for environmental protection and sustainable development, urging the implementation of the Kyoto Protocol by September. In a show of international cooperation, the two countries have united to encourage other nations to ratify and enforce the protocol, demonstrating their commitment to combating climate change and protecting the planet. With the Rio+10 meeting approaching, Brazil and Germany intend to present common positions, emphasizing the importance of the PPG-7 program and Germany's participation in protecting tropical forests. Their partnership highlights the global effort needed to address environmental challenges and promote sustainable development practices. Brazil and Germany's initiative serves as a call to action for the international community to prioritize environmental protection and work together towards a greener future.
END OF SUMMARY.
