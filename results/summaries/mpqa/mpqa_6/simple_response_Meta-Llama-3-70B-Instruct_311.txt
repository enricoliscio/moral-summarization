SUMMARY:

In a joint effort, Brazil and Germany are urging the implementation of the Kyoto Protocol by September, ahead of the Sustainable Development Summit in South Africa. Through their Partnership Brasil-Germany: Action Plan, they are calling on other countries to ratify and enforce the protocol, emphasizing the importance of cooperation in environmental and sustainable development efforts. The two nations also plan to present a united front at the Rio+10 meeting, further solidifying their commitment to protecting the environment. Notably, they highlighted the significance of the Pilot Program for Protection of Tropical Forests (PPG-7) and Germany's participation in the program. By taking a proactive stance, Brazil and Germany are setting a moral example for other countries to follow, demonstrating their responsibility towards the planet and future generations.

END OF SUMMARY.
