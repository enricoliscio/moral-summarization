SUMMARY:
In a joint statement titled "Partnership Brasil-Germany: Action Plan", Brazil and Germany have expressed their intention to see the Kyoto Protocol implemented by September during the Sustainable Development Summit (Rio+10) in South Africa. Both nations are encouraging other countries to ratify and enforce the protocol. The Action Plan also highlights the importance of cooperation between Brazil and Germany in the areas of environmental protection and sustainable development. They plan to present unified positions at Rio+10, emphasizing the significance of the Pilot Program for the Protection of Tropical Forests (PPG-7) and German participation in this initiative. This demonstrates a commitment to international cooperation and the moral imperative to take action on environmental sustainability and development.
