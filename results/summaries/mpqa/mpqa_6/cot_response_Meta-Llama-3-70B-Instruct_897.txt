SUMMARY:
Brazil and Germany have formed a partnership to promote sustainable development and environmental protection. In their joint document, "Partnership Brasil-Germany: Action Plan", they express their interest in seeing the Kyoto Protocol take effect by the Sustainable Development Summit (Rio+10) in September. They call on other countries to ratify and enforce the protocol, emphasizing the importance of cooperation in this area. The two countries also intend to expand their cooperation in environment and sustainable development, presenting common positions at the Rio+10 meeting. Furthermore, they highlight the importance of the Pilot Program for Protection of Tropical Forests (PPG-7) and Germany's participation in it. By working together, Brazil and Germany aim to promote sustainable development and protect the environment.
