SUMMARY:

Brazil and Germany have issued a joint document, "Partnership Brasil-Germany: Action Plan", calling for the implementation of the Kyoto Protocol by the Sustainable Development Summit (Rio+10) in September. They urge other countries to ratify and enforce the protocol. The document also outlines plans to expand cooperation on environmental and sustainable development issues. Brazil and Germany aim to present a united front at the Rio+10 meeting. Additionally, the document highlights the importance of the Pilot Program for Protection of Tropical Forests (PPG-7) and Germany's participation in the program. The partnership seeks to promote sustainable development and environmental protection.

END OF SUMMARY.
