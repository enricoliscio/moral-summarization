SUMMARY: In a joint document, Brazil and Germany have expressed their intention to see the Kyoto Protocol implemented by the Sustainable Development Summit (Rio+10) in September 2002. Both countries also urge other nations to ratify and work towards enforcing the protocol. The document highlights their commitment to expanding cooperation in the areas of environment and sustainable development and presents their plan to put forward common positions at the Rio+10 meeting. Additionally, it emphasizes the significance of the Pilot Program for Protection of Tropical Forests (PPG-7) and Germany's involvement in the program. The two countries are working together to address environmental concerns and promote sustainable practices.

END OF SUMMARY.
