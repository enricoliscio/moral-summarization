SUMMARY:

Brazil and Germany have joined forces to promote sustainable development and environmental protection. In their Partnership Brasil-Germany: Action Plan, they urge other countries to ratify and implement the Kyoto Protocol by the Sustainable Development Summit (Rio+10) in September. This collaborative effort aims to expand cooperation in environmental protection and sustainable development, with both nations presenting a united front at the Rio+10 meeting. The document highlights the significance of the Pilot Program for Protection of Tropical Forests (PPG-7), which Germany is participating in. By working together, Brazil and Germany hope to accelerate progress towards a more sustainable future, emphasizing the importance of protection and development for the well-being of our planet.

END OF SUMMARY.
