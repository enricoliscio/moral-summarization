SUMMARY:
Brazil and Germany have formed a partnership to take action towards sustainable development, calling for the implementation of the Kyoto Protocol by the Rio+10 summit in September. They urge other countries to ratify and enforce the protocol, emphasizing the importance of cooperation in the area of environment and sustainable development. Both countries plan to present common positions at the Rio+10 meeting, highlighting their commitment to sustainable development. The partnership also aims to expand cooperation in environmental protection, particularly through the Pilot Program for Protection of Tropical Forests (PPG-7), with German participation. This joint effort demonstrates their dedication to protecting the environment and promoting sustainable development. END OF SUMMARY.
