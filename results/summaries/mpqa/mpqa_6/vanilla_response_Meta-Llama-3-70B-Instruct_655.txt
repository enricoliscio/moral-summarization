SUMMARY:

Brazil and Germany have jointly called for the implementation of the Kyoto Protocol by September, ahead of the Sustainable Development Summit (Rio+10) in South Africa. The two countries have expressed their commitment to the protocol and urged other nations to ratify and enforce it. In a document titled "Partnership Brasil-Germany: Action Plan", they also announced plans to expand cooperation on environmental and sustainable development issues. Brazil and Germany aim to present a unified stance at the Rio+10 meeting. Additionally, the document highlights the importance of the Pilot Program for Protection of Tropical Forests (PPG-7) and Germany's participation in the program. The partnership seeks to promote sustainable development and environmental protection.

END OF SUMMARY.
