SUMMARY:
Brazil and Germany have formed a Partnership to promote sustainable development and environmental protection. In their Action Plan, they urge countries to implement the Kyoto Protocol by the Rio+10 Sustainable Development Summit in September. They call on nations to ratify and enforce the protocol, emphasizing its Importance in combating climate change. The two countries also plan to expand cooperation in environmental protection and present common positions at the Rio+10 meeting. Furthermore, they highlight the significance of the Pilot Program for Protection of Tropical Forests (PPG-7) and Germany's participation in it. By working together, Brazil and Germany aim to promote sustainable development and protect the environment for future generations.
END OF SUMMARY.
