SUMMARY:
Brazil and Germany have formed a partnership to promote sustainable development and environmental protection. In their joint document, "Partnership Brasil-Germany: Action Plan", they express interest in seeing the Kyoto Protocol take effect by the Sustainable Development Summit (Rio+10) in September. They call on other countries to ratify and enforce the protocol, emphasizing the importance of cooperation in this area. The two countries intend to present common positions at the Rio+10 meeting and expand their cooperation in environment and sustainable development. They also highlight the significance of the Pilot Program for Protection of Tropical Forests (PPG-7) and German participation in the program. By working together, Brazil and Germany aim to promote a more sustainable future.
