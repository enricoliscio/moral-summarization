SUMMARY: Brazil and Germany have released a joint statement expressing their desire for the Kyoto Protocol to be implemented by September during the Sustainable Development Summit in South Africa. The two countries also urged other nations to ratify and enforce the protocol. The statement, titled "Partnership Brasil-Germany: Action Plan," highlights their intention to strengthen cooperation in environmental and sustainable development areas. Brazil and Germany plan to present united fronts at the Rio+10 meeting and emphasize the significance of the PPG-7 program for protecting tropical forests, with German involvement. The countries' shared goal is to have the Kyoto Protocol in effect by the summit, showcasing their commitment to environmental sustainability and global cooperation.

END OF SUMMARY.
