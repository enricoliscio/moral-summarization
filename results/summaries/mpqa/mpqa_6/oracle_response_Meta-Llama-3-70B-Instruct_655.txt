SUMMARY: Brazil and Germany have joined forces to promote sustainable development and environmental protection through the implementation of the Kyoto Protocol by September's Sustainable Development Summit in South Africa. The two countries urge other nations to ratify and enforce the protocol, emphasizing the importance of cooperation in the area of environment and sustainable development. They plan to present a united front at the Rio+10 meeting, highlighting their commitment to protecting the planet. The Partnership Brasil-Germany: Action Plan also underscores the significance of the Pilot Program for Protection of Tropical Forests (PPG-7), with Germany's participation being a key aspect of their joint effort. By working together, Brazil and Germany aim to drive progress towards a more sustainable future, where development and protection of the environment go hand in hand. END OF SUMMARY.
