SUMMARY:
Brazil and Germany have released a joint document, "Partnership Brasil-Germany: Action Plan", calling for the implementation of the Kyoto Protocol by the Sustainable Development Summit (Rio+10) in September. The document emphasizes the importance of ratifying and enforcing the protocol, and both countries intend to expand their cooperation in the area of environment and sustainable development. They plan to present common positions at the Rio+10 meeting, highlighting the significance of the Pilot Program for Protection of Tropical Forests (PPG-7) and Germany's participation in the program. By working together, Brazil and Germany aim to promote sustainable development and protect the environment, demonstrating their commitment to a common goal. END OF SUMMARY.
