SUMMARY:

In a joint effort, Brazil and Germany are urging the international community to implement the Kyoto Protocol by September's Sustainable Development Summit in South Africa. Through their Partnership Brasil-Germany: Action Plan, they are calling on other countries to ratify and enforce the protocol, which aims to combat climate change. The two nations also plan to strengthen their cooperation on environmental and sustainable development issues, presenting a united front at the Rio+10 meeting. Furthermore, they emphasized the importance of the Pilot Program for Protection of Tropical Forests, with Germany actively participating in the initiative. This partnership demonstrates a moral commitment to protecting the environment and promoting sustainable development, setting an example for other nations to follow. By working together, Brazil and Germany are sending a strong message about the need for collective action to address the pressing issue of climate change.

END OF SUMMARY.
