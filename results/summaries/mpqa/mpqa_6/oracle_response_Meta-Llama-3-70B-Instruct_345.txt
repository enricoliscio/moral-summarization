SUMMARY: Brazil and Germany have joined forces to promote sustainable development and environmental protection through the implementation of the Kyoto Protocol by September. The two countries have expressed their commitment to seeing the protocol take effect by the Sustainable Development Summit (Rio+10) in South Africa. They are urging other countries to ratify and enforce the protocol, emphasizing the importance of cooperation in the area of environment and sustainable development. The Partnership Brasil-Germany: Action Plan also highlights the significance of the Pilot Program for Protection of Tropical Forests (PPG-7) and Germany's participation in the program. By working together, Brazil and Germany aim to present common positions at the Rio+10 meeting, prioritizing the protection of the environment and promoting sustainable development. END OF SUMMARY.
