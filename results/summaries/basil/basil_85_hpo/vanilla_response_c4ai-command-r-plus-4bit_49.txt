SUMMARY: President Donald Trump criticized social media companies for allegedly discriminating against Republican and conservative voices on their platforms. He suggested that the solution is to let everyone participate, regardless of their views, and that people should be able to express their opinions freely online. Trump accused social media companies of censorship and of favoring liberal voices over conservative ones. He also criticized news media outlets like CNN and MSNBC, referring to them as "fake news." The President's comments come amidst recent actions taken by social media platforms to ban conspiracy theorist Alex Jones and his website Infowars. Trump's stance highlights ongoing concerns about bias and censorship in the moderation practices of social media companies.
END OF SUMMARY.
