SUMMARY: President Donald Trump took to Twitter to criticize social media companies for allegedly discriminating against Republican and conservative voices. He suggested that the solution is to let everyone participate, regardless of their views, and that society will have to figure out how to deal with the consequences. Trump accused social media platforms of censoring right-wing voices while letting others remain. He also criticized mainstream media outlets like CNN and MSNBC, calling them fake news. Trump's comments come amid recent actions by tech companies to ban conspiracy theorist Alex Jones and his website Infowars from their platforms. Trump's tweet highlights the ongoing debate about free speech and the role of social media platforms in policing content.
