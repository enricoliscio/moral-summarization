SUMMARY: President Donald Trump has taken to Twitter to criticize social media companies for their alleged bias against conservative voices, suggesting that they are engaging in dangerous censorship. While acknowledging that both good and bad voices exist, Trump emphasizes the importance of allowing everyone to participate and determine right from wrong for themselves. He accuses the companies of selectively silencing right-wing individuals while turning a blind eye to those on the left, specifically calling out CNN and MSNBC for spreading fake news. Though Trump does not mention any specific individuals, his comments come amidst the recent banning of controversial figure Alex Jones and Infowars from several platforms. Trump's stance highlights a moral concern for freedom of expression and the potential dangers of censorship, advocating for an inclusive approach where all perspectives are heard and individuals are trusted to discern the truth.
END OF SUMMARY.
