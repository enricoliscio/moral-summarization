SUMMARY: President Trump took to Twitter to accuse social media companies of discriminating against Republican/Conservative voices, claiming that they are destroying too many voices and closing down the opinions of those on the right. He proposed a solution of letting everybody participate, good and bad, and figuring it out together. Trump also criticized the tech giants for doing nothing to others while engaging in censorship, which he called dangerous and impossible to police. The president's rant included familiar targets like fake news networks CNN and MSNBC, as well as the recent banning of conspiracy theorist Alex Jones and Infowars from various platforms. Trump's tweets highlight ongoing concerns about the role of social media in shaping political discourse and the potential for censorship and discrimination.
END OF SUMMARY.
