SUMMARY: President Trump took to Twitter to criticize Kathy Griffin, a television comedian, and Democrats in Congress. Griffin faced backlash for a video showing her holding what looked like the bloody, severed head of Trump. The video sparked outrage across the political spectrum, leading Griffin to apologize and CNN to fire her from their New Year's Eve program. Melania Trump also spoke out against Griffin, questioning her mental health. Separately, Trump accused Democrats of trying to suppress testimony related to the investigation into Russian meddling in the 2016 election. He claimed that Carter Page, a former Trump campaign adviser, wanted to testify but was being blocked by Democrats. Page alleged that Comey and Brennan gave false testimony and called for an investigation into collusion between Clinton campaign associates and Obama administration officials. 

END OF SUMMARY
