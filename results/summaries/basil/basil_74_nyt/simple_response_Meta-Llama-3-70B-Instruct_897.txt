SUMMARY:

President Trump has lashed out at comedian Kathy Griffin and Democrats on Twitter, sparking controversy and outrage. Griffin posed for a video holding a severed and bloodied head resembling Trump, prompting widespread criticism and an apology from her. The First Lady, Melania Trump, questioned Griffin's sanity, calling the photo opportunity "wrong" and "disturbing". CNN fired Griffin from their New Year's Eve program. Trump also accused Democrats of trying to suppress testimony involving the Russian election meddling investigation, calling it a "witch hunt" against him. He defended Carter Page, a former Trump campaign adviser, who wants to testify and clear his name. The incident highlights the divisive and inflammatory nature of Trump's presidency, with many condemning Griffin's actions as inappropriate and Trump's response as aggressive and divisive.
