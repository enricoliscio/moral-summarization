SUMMARY: President Trump castigated comedian Kathy Griffin for posing with what looked like his severed head, sparking protests and causing Ms. Griffin to apologize. The Trumps called the photo "disturbing" and questioned Ms. Griffin's sanity. Ms. Griffin admitted that she "crossed the line" and "went too far," and CNN fired her from her annual New Year's Eve program. In other news, Mr. Trump accused Democrats of trying to "squelch" testimony in the Russia investigation, calling it a "witch hunt." Carter Page, a former Trump adviser, wanted to testify to clear his name and expose "false or misleading" statements by James Comey and John Brennan. Mr. Page urged the investigation of "collusion" between Clinton campaign associates and Obama officials.
