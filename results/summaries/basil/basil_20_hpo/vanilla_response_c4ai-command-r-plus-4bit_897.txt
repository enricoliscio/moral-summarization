SUMMARY: In recent interviews and speeches, Republican presidential candidate Rick Santorum has made several controversial statements regarding prenatal testing, public education, and President Obama's ideology. Santorum defended his claim that prenatal testing leads to more abortions, suggesting that insurance providers should not be forced to cover these procedures. He also criticized federally provided education, stating that it is "anachronistic" and should be customizable like buying a car. Additionally, Santorum claimed that Obama's policies are based on a "phony theology" that prioritizes the environment over human needs. He clarified that he was referring to radical environmentalists and their worldview, not questioning Obama's faith. Obama campaign aide Robert Gibbs criticized Santorum's rhetoric as "well over the line." Santorum's comments have sparked debate and criticism from various quarters.
END OF SUMMARY.
