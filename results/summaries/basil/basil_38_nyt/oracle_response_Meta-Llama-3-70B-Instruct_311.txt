SUMMARY:

The Treasury Department and the Internal Revenue Service have announced a landmark ruling, ensuring that all same-sex couples who are legally married will be recognized as such for federal tax purposes, regardless of the state they live in. This decision treats gay couples in the same way as straight couples, providing certainty and clear guidance for all legally married same-sex couples nationwide. The ruling assures these couples that they can move freely throughout the country, knowing their federal filing status will not change. Gay and civil rights groups have praised the decision, stating that committed and loving gay and lesbian married couples will now be treated equally under federal tax laws, regardless of their state of residence. This ruling marks a significant step towards fairness and ending discrimination against same-sex couples.

END OF SUMMARY.
