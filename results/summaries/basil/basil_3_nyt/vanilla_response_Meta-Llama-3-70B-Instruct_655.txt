SUMMARY:

Representative Bob Etheridge, a Democrat from North Carolina, apologized for physically confronting a young man who asked if he supported President Obama's agenda. The incident, caught on video, showed Etheridge grabbing the man's hand, arm, and neck, and demanding to know his identity. The video was posted online and received widespread attention. Etheridge stated that he "deeply and profoundly" regrets his reaction and apologizes to all involved, emphasizing his commitment to promoting civil public discourse. The incident has sparked criticism from Republicans, with one spokesman calling Etheridge's conduct "unbecoming of a member of Congress." Etheridge, who faces a re-election challenge, has a history of winning his district by a significant margin.

END OF SUMMARY.
