SUMMARY:

Democratic Representative Bob Etheridge of North Carolina apologized for physically confronting a young man who asked if he supported President Obama's agenda. The incident, caught on video, shows Etheridge grabbing the man's hand, arm, and neck, and demanding to know his identity. Etheridge expressed regret, stating that he has always tried to treat people with respect, regardless of their viewpoints. He acknowledged that his reaction was poor and vowed to promote civil public discourse. The video was circulated by conservative groups, who criticized Etheridge's behavior as "unbecoming of a member of Congress." The incident has sparked controversy, with some questioning Etheridge's fitness for office. The apology and criticism highlight the importance of maintaining respect and civility in political interactions, even in the face of partisan disagreements.

END OF SUMMARY.
