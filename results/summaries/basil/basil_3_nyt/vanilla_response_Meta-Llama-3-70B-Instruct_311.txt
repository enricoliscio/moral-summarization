SUMMARY:

Representative Bob Etheridge, a Democrat from North Carolina, apologized for physically confronting a young man who asked if he supported President Obama's agenda. The incident was captured on video and showed Etheridge grabbing the man by the hand, arm, and neck. Etheridge stated that he "deeply and profoundly regrets" his reaction and apologized to all involved, emphasizing the importance of civil public discourse. The video was posted on a conservative website and circulated by the National Republican Congressional Committee, sparking criticism from Republicans. Etheridge, who faces a re-election challenge, won his last election by 36 percentage points and is not considered a top Republican target.

END OF SUMMARY.
