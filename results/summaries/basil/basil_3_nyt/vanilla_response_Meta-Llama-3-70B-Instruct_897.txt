SUMMARY:

Representative Bob Etheridge, a Democrat from North Carolina, apologized for physically confronting a young man who asked if he supported President Obama's agenda. The incident was caught on video and showed Etheridge grabbing the man's hand, arm, and neck. Etheridge claimed he regretted his reaction and would work to promote civil public discourse. The video was posted on a conservative website and circulated by the National Republican Congressional Committee, sparking criticism from Republicans. Etheridge, a seven-term House member, faces a re-election challenge in a conservative district. The incident has drawn attention to his conduct, with Republicans calling it "unbecoming of a member of Congress."

END OF SUMMARY.
