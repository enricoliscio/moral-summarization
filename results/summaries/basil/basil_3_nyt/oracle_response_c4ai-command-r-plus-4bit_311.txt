SUMMARY: Representative Bob Etheridge has apologized for his poor conduct after grabbing a young man by the hand, arm and neck as he tried to ask the congressman whether he supports President Obama's agenda. The incident, which was captured on video, shows the congressman demanding to know the young man's identity and then grabbing his camera. The video received widespread attention and was criticized by the National Republican Congressional Committee as "unbecoming of a member of Congress." Mr. Etheridge acknowledged that his actions were not in line with the respect and civil public discourse that he has always tried to promote during his years of service. He issued a statement expressing his deep regret and apologizing to all involved. The incident highlights the partisan nature of politics and the importance of maintaining a respectful dialogue, even in the face of intrusive questioning. END OF SUMMARY.
