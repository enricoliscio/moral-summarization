SUMMARY:

Democratic Representative Bob Etheridge of North Carolina has apologized for physically confronting a young man who asked him if he supports President Obama's agenda. The incident, caught on video, shows Etheridge grabbing the man's hand, arm, and neck, and demanding to know his identity. Etheridge has since expressed deep regret for his actions, stating that he has always tried to treat people with respect, regardless of their viewpoints. He emphasized the importance of promoting civil public discourse. The video was widely circulated by conservative groups, with Republicans condemning Etheridge's behavior as "unbecoming of a member of Congress." The incident has sparked controversy, highlighting the need for respectful dialogue in politics. Etheridge's apology is a step towards rectifying his mistake and upholding the values of civility and respect in public discourse.
