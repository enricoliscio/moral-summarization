A new political advertisement targets Jewish populations in Florida and attempts to stoke anxiety about a nuclear Iran, taking a swipe at President Obama by criticizing his foreign policy as "apologetic". The ad raises moral questions about the use of fear and stokes anxiety in political campaigns, as well as the ethics of using a foreign leader's words to criticize a sitting US president.
