Glenn Beck, a conservative icon, delivered a powerful speech at the Conservative Political Action Conference, denouncing progressivism as a "cancer" that must be eradicated from the system. He also stressed that both parties are to blame, with Democrats taxing and spending, and Republicans simply spending. Beck's message was clear: progressivism is the root of America's problems, and it's time to take responsibility and make a change. The audience was enthralled by his personal anecdotes and philosophical riffs, and his call to action was met with wild applause.
