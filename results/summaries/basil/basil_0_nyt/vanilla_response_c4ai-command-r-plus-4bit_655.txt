SUMMARY: Glenn Beck, a hero of the political right, delivered a rousing speech at the Conservative Political Action Conference, denouncing progressivism as a cancer and calling for a return to personal responsibility and fiscal conservatism. Beck criticized both Democrats and Republicans for their spending habits and lack of direction, stating that the Republican Party needs to admit its problems and recommit to its values. He emphasized the importance of individual happiness and success, arguing that conservatives believe individuals should not be penalized for their financial achievements. Beck's speech, which combined his personal life with his political philosophy, captivated the audience and highlighted his belief in small government and individual responsibility.
