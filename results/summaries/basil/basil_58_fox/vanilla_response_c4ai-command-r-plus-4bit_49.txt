SUMMARY: New Jersey Governor Chris Christie is expected to announce his candidacy for the 2016 Republican presidential nomination, joining a crowded field of contenders. Despite facing challenges such as low poll numbers, fundraising deficits, and scandals, Christie is known for his brash and combative personality, which he will likely leverage as a key asset in his campaign. He is particularly skilled at town hall meetings, often leaving attendees with strong emotional responses. While his popularity has waned, and many mega-donors have shifted their support to rival candidates, Christie remains a formidable contender, and his entry into the race adds further intrigue to the already competitive Republican primary.
END OF SUMMARY.
