SUMMARY: New Jersey Governor Chris Christie is expected to announce his candidacy for the 2016 Republican presidential nomination. Despite facing challenges such as low poll numbers, fundraising deficits, and scandals, Christie is known for his brash and combative personality, which could be his main asset in the race. He is particularly skilled at town hall meetings, often leaving people laughing, weeping, or hoping for more. While some mega-donors have drifted towards his rivals, Christie still has a chance to make an impact in the race with his unique style and ability to connect with people.
END OF SUMMARY.
