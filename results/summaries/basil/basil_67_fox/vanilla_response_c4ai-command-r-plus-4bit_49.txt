SUMMARY: Former Mexican President Vicente Fox and Republican nominee Donald Trump engaged in a heated exchange on Twitter ahead of Trump's visit to Mexico. Fox expressed his disapproval of current President Enrique Pena Nieto's decision to invite Trump, stating that Trump is "not welcome" in Mexico due to his offensive remarks. He criticized Pena Nieto's decision as a "big mistake" that could be seen as a betrayal. In response, Trump took to Twitter to highlight that Fox had previously invited him after apologizing for using profanity in reference to Trump's wall proposal. Fox then fired back, demanding an apology from Trump and accusing him of lying. This exchange reflects the tense relationship between the two figures and the controversy surrounding Trump's visit to Mexico.
