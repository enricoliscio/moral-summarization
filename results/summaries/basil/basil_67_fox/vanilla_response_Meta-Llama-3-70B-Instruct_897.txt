SUMMARY:

Former Mexican President Vicente Fox and Donald Trump engaged in a Twitter feud before Trump's visit to Mexico on Wednesday. Fox criticized Trump's visit, stating that he is "not welcome" in Mexico and that President Enrique Pena Nieto's decision to invite him was a "big mistake." Fox also called Trump's visit an "offense" and said that Pena Nieto would be seen as a "traitor" for hosting him. Trump responded on Twitter, reminding Fox that he had apologized for using profanity when referring to Trump's proposed border wall. Fox fired back, demanding an apology from Trump and accusing him of lying. The exchange highlights the tense relationship between Trump and Mexico, with Fox being a vocal critic of Trump's policies and rhetoric. The visit is seen as a significant event in the presidential campaign, with Trump seeking to improve his image on the international stage.
