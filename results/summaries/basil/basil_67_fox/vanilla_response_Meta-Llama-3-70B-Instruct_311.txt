SUMMARY:

Former Mexican President Vicente Fox and Donald Trump engaged in a Twitter feud ahead of Trump's visit to Mexico. Fox, a vocal critic of Trump, expressed his disagreement with current President Enrique Pena Nieto's decision to invite Trump to meet with him, stating that Trump is "not welcome" in Mexico. Fox criticized Trump's past comments about Mexico and Mexicans, and warned that Pena Nieto's decision could be seen as a "big mistake" and even considered "traitorous". Trump responded on Twitter, referencing a past incident where Fox apologized for using profanity when discussing Trump's proposed border wall. Fox fired back, demanding an apology and accusing Trump of lying. The exchange highlights the tense relationship between Trump and Mexico, and raises questions about the purpose and outcome of Trump's visit.
