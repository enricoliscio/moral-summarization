SUMMARY:

Donald Trump's visit to Mexico sparked a Twitter feud with former Mexican President Vicente Fox. Fox criticized current President Enrique Pena Nieto's decision to invite Trump, saying he's "not welcome" in Mexico and that his visit is a "big mistake." Fox also called Trump out for his past offensive comments about Mexico and its people. Trump responded on Twitter, bringing up a past incident where Fox apologized for using profanity to describe Trump's proposed border wall. Fox fired back, demanding an apology and accusing Trump of lying. The exchange highlights the tension surrounding Trump's visit, which has been met with protests and criticism from many in Mexico.

END OF SUMMARY.
