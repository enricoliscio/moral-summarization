SUMMARY:

Donald Trump's visit to Mexico sparked a Twitter feud with former Mexican President Vicente Fox, who declared the Republican nominee "not welcome" in the country. Fox, a vocal critic of Trump, criticized current President Enrique Pena Nieto's decision to host Trump, calling it a "big mistake" that would make him seem like a "traitor". Fox expressed outrage, saying Mexicans are "offended" by Trump's words and don't want him in the country. Trump fired back, accusing Fox of hypocrisy for apologizing for using profanity to describe Trump's proposed border wall. Fox responded, demanding Trump apologize and "stop lying". The exchange highlights the contentious nature of Trump's visit, with Fox railing against the decision to host him.
