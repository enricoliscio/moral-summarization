SUMMARY:

Former Mexican President Vicente Fox criticized current President Enrique Pena Nieto's decision to invite Donald Trump to Mexico, saying Trump is "not welcome" in the country. Fox stated that Trump's visit is a "big mistake" and that Pena Nieto will be seen as a "traitor" for hosting him. Trump responded on Twitter, referencing a past incident where Fox apologized for using an expletive to describe Trump's proposed border wall. Fox fired back on Twitter, demanding Trump apologize and "stop lying." The exchange escalated into a Twitter war between the two.

END OF SUMMARY.
