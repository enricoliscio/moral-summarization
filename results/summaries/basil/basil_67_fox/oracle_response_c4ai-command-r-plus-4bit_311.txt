SUMMARY: Former Mexican President Vicente Fox engaged in a Twitter war with Donald Trump, expressing his strong disapproval of Trump's visit to Mexico and his meeting with President Enrique Pena Nieto. Fox's moral indignation was evident as he asserted that Trump "is not welcome" in Mexico and that the Mexican people "reject his visit." He further criticized President Pena Nieto for hosting Trump, suggesting that it was a "big mistake" and could be seen as a betrayal. The exchange escalated when Trump fired back on Twitter, accusing Fox of being hypocritical by inviting him to Mexico despite their previous disagreements. Fox vehemently denied this claim and demanded an apology from Trump, accusing him of lying. The dispute highlights the ethical and political tensions surrounding Trump's visit to Mexico, with Fox's passionate defense of Mexican interests taking center stage.
