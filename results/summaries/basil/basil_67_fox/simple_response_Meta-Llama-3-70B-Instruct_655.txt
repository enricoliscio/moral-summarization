SUMMARY:

In a heated Twitter exchange, former Mexican President Vicente Fox slammed Donald Trump's visit to Mexico, calling him "not welcome" and accusing current President Enrique Pena Nieto of taking a "big political risk" by hosting him. Fox condemned Trump's past offensive remarks, saying Mexico rejects his visit and doesn't want him. Trump retaliated, bringing up Fox's past apology for using profanity when referring to Trump's proposed border wall. Fox fired back, demanding an apology and accusing Trump of lying. The exchange highlights the deep-seated tensions and mistrust between Trump and Mexico, with Fox's strong words reflecting the country's outrage and sense of disrespect. The incident raises questions about the wisdom of Trump's visit and the potential consequences of his divisive rhetoric.
