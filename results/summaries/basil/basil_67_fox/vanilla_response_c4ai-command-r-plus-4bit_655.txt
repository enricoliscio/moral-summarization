SUMMARY: Former Mexican President Vicente Fox and Donald Trump engaged in a Twitter war ahead of Trump's visit to Mexico. Fox criticized current President Enrique Pena Nieto's decision to invite Trump, stating that he is "not welcome" in Mexico due to his offensive remarks. He further warned that Pena Nieto risks being seen as a "traitor" for hosting Trump. Trump retaliated on Twitter, claiming that Fox had invited him after apologizing for using profanity in a previous comment about Trump's wall proposal. Fox responded by demanding an apology from Trump and calling him a liar. The exchange highlights the tension surrounding Trump's visit to Mexico and the controversial nature of his relationship with the country.
END OF SUMMARY.
