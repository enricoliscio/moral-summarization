SUMMARY: Former Mexican President Vicente Fox and Republican nominee Donald Trump engaged in a Twitter war after Fox expressed his disagreement with current President Enrique Pena Nieto's decision to invite Trump to Mexico. Fox stated that Trump was "not welcome" in Mexico due to his offensive remarks and that Pena Nieto's invitation was a "big mistake." Trump responded on Twitter, claiming that Fox had invited him after apologizing for using profanity in a previous comment about Trump's wall proposal. Fox fired back, demanding an apology from Trump and calling him a liar. The exchange highlights the tension between Trump and Mexican officials over his controversial remarks and policies regarding Mexico.
