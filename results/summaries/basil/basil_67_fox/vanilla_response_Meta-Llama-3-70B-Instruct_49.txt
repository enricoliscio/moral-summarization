SUMMARY:

Donald Trump's visit to Mexico sparked a Twitter feud with former Mexican President Vicente Fox. Fox criticized current President Enrique Pena Nieto's decision to invite Trump, saying he is "not welcome" in Mexico and that his visit is a "big mistake." Fox also called Trump out for his past offensive comments about Mexico and its people. Trump responded on Twitter, bringing up a past incident where Fox apologized for using profanity to describe Trump's proposed border wall. Fox fired back, demanding an apology from Trump and accusing him of lying. The exchange highlights the tense relations between Trump and Mexico, which have been strained since Trump's comments about Mexican immigrants during his presidential campaign announcement.

END OF SUMMARY.
