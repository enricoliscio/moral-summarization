SUMMARY:

Former Mexican President Vicente Fox sparked a Twitter feud with Donald Trump, declaring the Republican nominee "not welcome" in Mexico. Fox criticized current President Enrique Pena Nieto's decision to invite Trump to meet with him, saying it was a "big mistake" and that Trump's visit would be seen as an "offense". Trump responded on Twitter, bringing up a past incident where Fox apologized for using profanity to reject paying for a border wall. Fox fired back, demanding Trump apologize and stop lying. The exchange highlights the deep-seated tensions and mistrust between Trump and Mexico, with Fox standing up for his country's dignity and rejecting Trump's divisive rhetoric.

END OF SUMMARY.
