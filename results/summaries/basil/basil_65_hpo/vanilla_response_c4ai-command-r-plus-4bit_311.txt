SUMMARY: Former New York mayor and billionaire businessman Michael Bloomberg spoke at the Democratic National Convention on Wednesday in support of Hillary Clinton. In his speech, Bloomberg criticized Republican nominee Donald Trump, calling him a "dangerous demagogue" and arguing that he would be a disastrous president. Bloomberg highlighted Trump's business failures and hypocrisies, stating that he left a trail of bankruptcies, lawsuits, and dissatisfied customers and contractors. He also mocked Trump's claims of being a great businessman, noting that he inherited his wealth from his father. Bloomberg, a former Republican himself, praised Clinton as a "sane, competent person" and urged Americans to vote for her. He warned that a Trump presidency would harm the country economically and internationally, and endanger the safety of American communities.
END OF SUMMARY.
