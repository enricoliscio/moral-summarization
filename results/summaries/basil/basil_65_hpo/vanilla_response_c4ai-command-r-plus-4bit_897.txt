SUMMARY: Former New York Mayor Michael Bloomberg, a billionaire and former Republican, endorsed Hillary Clinton at the Democratic National Convention, criticizing Trump as a dangerous demagogue and a failed businessman. Bloomberg highlighted Trump's bankruptcies, lawsuits, and dissatisfied customers and stockholders, arguing that Trump is not a competent leader. Bloomberg also mocked Trump's claims of business success, noting that he started his own business without his father's financial support. Bloomberg warned that a Trump presidency would damage the economy, retirement savings, and America's global influence, urging Americans to vote for Clinton as a sane and competent alternative. Bloomberg's speech, which included unscripted remarks, emphasized Trump's hypocrisy and the potential disasters a Trump presidency could bring.
