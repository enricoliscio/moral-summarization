SUMMARY: Donald Trump criticizes Supreme Court Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg's comments on his presidential campaign, calling them "highly inappropriate" and suggesting she owes the court an apology. Ginsburg expressed fear about the prospect of a Trump presidency. Trump calls her remarks "beneath the court" and suggests she should step down. Separately, Trump discusses his vice-presidential selection process, stating that he will announce his choice by Friday. He mentions potential candidates, including Chris Christie, Newt Gingrich, Mike Flynn, Joni Ernst, and Mike Pence, praising each for their strengths. Trump emphasizes that his decision will be based on his instincts and experience with the candidates. He also mentions his optimism about winning Democratic states like Connecticut.
END OF SUMMARY.
