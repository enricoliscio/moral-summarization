SUMMARY: Donald Trump criticized Supreme Court Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg for her comments about his presidential campaign, suggesting that her remarks were inappropriate and that she should apologize. Ginsburg had expressed fear about the prospect of a Trump presidency and joked about moving to New Zealand if he were elected. Trump called Ginsburg's comments "a disgrace to the court" and suggested she should step down from her position. Trump also discussed his ongoing search for a running mate, stating that he would be making a decision within the next few days and that his choice would be based on instinct. He mentioned several candidates he is considering, including Governor Chris Christie, Newt Gingrich, Governor Mike Pence, and Senator Joni Ernst. Trump also expressed confidence in his ability to win over traditionally Democratic states like Connecticut.
END OF SUMMARY.
