SUMMARY: President Trump praised FBI special counsel Robert Mueller for disputing a BuzzFeed report that claimed Trump directed Michael Cohen to lie to Congress about a proposed Trump Tower project in Moscow. Mueller's office released a statement saying that BuzzFeed's description of specific statements and characterization of documents were "not accurate." Trump called the BuzzFeed piece a "disgrace to journalism" and criticized the mainstream media's coverage of the story. BuzzFeed stood by its reporting and urged Mueller to clarify what was being disputed. Trump's lawyer Rudy Giuliani also praised Mueller's office for correcting the story and criticized the press for its "hysterical desire to destroy" the President. A BuzzFeed spokesman defended the organization's reporting, stating that they remain confident in their story.
END OF SUMMARY.
