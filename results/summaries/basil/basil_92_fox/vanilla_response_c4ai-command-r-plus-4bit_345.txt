SUMMARY: President Trump praised FBI special counsel Robert Mueller for disputing a BuzzFeed news report claiming Trump told Michael Cohen to lie to Congress about a Trump Tower project in Moscow. Mueller's office released a statement saying BuzzFeed's description of specific statements and characterization of documents were "not accurate." Trump called the BuzzFeed piece a "disgrace to journalism" and criticized the mainstream media's coverage of the story. BuzzFeed editor-in-chief defended the reporting and urged Mueller to clarify his statement. Trump's lawyer, Rudy Giuliani, also praised Mueller's office for correcting the story and criticized the press for their "hysterical desire to destroy" Trump. BuzzFeed stood by its reporting, stating that they remain confident in their story.

END OF SUMMARY.
