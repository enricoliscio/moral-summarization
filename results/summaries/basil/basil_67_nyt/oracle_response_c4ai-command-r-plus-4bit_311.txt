SUMMARY: Former Mexican President Vicente Fox has publicly condemned Donald Trump's invitation to Mexico, calling it a mistake and urging Mexicans to reject Trump's visit. Fox criticized Trump's demonization of Mexicans and his inconsistent policies, arguing that he cannot be trusted. He emphasized that Mexico will not pay for Trump's proposed border wall and denounced Trump as a false prophet who is unfit to lead a nation. In response to Fox's statements, Trump took to Twitter, but Fox countered by reminding him of his previous invitation to apologize to Mexicans. Fox's message conveyed a strong moral stance against Trump's controversial actions and statements, emphasizing respect and trust.
