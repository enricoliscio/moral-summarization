SUMMARY: Former Mexican President Vicente Fox has publicly denounced Donald Trump and his visit to Mexico, calling it a mistake and stating that Trump is not welcome in the country. Fox criticized Trump's demonization of Mexicans and his inconsistent policy stances, arguing that he cannot be trusted and should never be granted the presidency. He also reaffirmed Mexico's refusal to pay for Trump's proposed border wall. In response to Trump's claims that he was invited by Fox, the former president clarified that he had invited Trump to apologize to Mexicans, not for a friendly visit. Fox's strong moral stance against Trump's divisive and disrespectful rhetoric highlights the ethical concerns surrounding the Republican nominee's campaign and the potential consequences for US-Mexico relations.
