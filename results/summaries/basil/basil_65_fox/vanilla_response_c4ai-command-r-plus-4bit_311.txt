SUMMARY: Former New York City Mayor Michael Bloomberg endorsed Hillary Clinton at the Democratic National Convention, calling it imperative that voters choose her over Donald Trump. Despite their disagreements, Bloomberg emphasized the importance of putting differences aside for the country's good. He criticized Trump as a "dangerous demagogue" and a "bomb-thrower," highlighting their contrasting business histories and Trump's record of bankruptcies and lawsuits. Bloomberg, who considered an independent presidential run, warned against Trump's economic plan and immigration policies, deeming them detrimental to the nation. He urged voters to unite behind Clinton to prevent a Trump presidency. Bloomberg's endorsement reflected their shared history in New York politics and his concern about the consequences of a Trump administration.
END OF SUMMARY.
