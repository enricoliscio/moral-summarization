SUMMARY: Former New York City Mayor Michael Bloomberg endorsed Hillary Clinton at the Democratic National Convention, urging voters to support her over Donald Trump. Bloomberg, a former Republican and independent, highlighted the importance of putting aside political disagreements for the good of the country. He criticized Trump as a "dangerous demagogue" and a "hypocrite," contrasting his own business success with Trump's record of bankruptcies and lawsuits. Bloomberg, who considered an independent presidential run, warned against Trump's immigration policies and economic plans, calling them a "con" and a threat to the nation's well-being. His endorsement highlighted the moral imperative to prioritize the country's best interests over personal differences, emphasizing the dangers of Trump's divisive and destructive agenda. Bloomberg's stance demonstrated a commitment to ethical leadership and the responsibility to prevent a potentially harmful presidency.
END OF SUMMARY.
