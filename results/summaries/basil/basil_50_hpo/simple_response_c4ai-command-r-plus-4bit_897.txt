SUMMARY: Susan Rice, Barack Obama's national security advisor, condemns Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu's plan to address Congress as "destructive" to US-Israel relations. This breach of diplomatic protocol, orchestrated by House Speaker John Boehner, has injected partisanship into a relationship that has always been bipartisan. Obama refuses to meet Netanyahu, citing the proximity to Israeli elections. Lawmakers worry about the impact on Iran nuclear talks. Patrick Leahy calls the speech a "stunt." The incident highlights the moral imperative of upholding diplomatic norms and the potential consequences of politicizing international relations. END OF SUMMARY.
