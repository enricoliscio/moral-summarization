SUMMARY:

Supreme Court Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg's recent comments about Donald Trump's presidency have raised questions about the propriety of her candor and the court's integrity. Critics contend that her comments are "beyond the pale" and that she should "refuse" to participate in such cases. Some argue that her actions have "taken sides" in the election, undermining the court's apolitical image.
