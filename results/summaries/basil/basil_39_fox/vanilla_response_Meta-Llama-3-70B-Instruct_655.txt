SUMMARY:

Mary Cheney, the openly lesbian daughter of former Vice President Dick Cheney, has publicly criticized her sister Elizabeth Cheney, a Wyoming Senate candidate, for her stance on same-sex marriage. Elizabeth Cheney believes the issue should be decided by the states and the people, not by judges or legislators. Mary Cheney, who has been married to her wife since 2012, disagrees, stating that "freedom means freedom for everyone" and that all families deserve the same rights and protections. Mary Cheney posted her criticism on Facebook, encouraging her followers to like and share if they agree. The disagreement is notable, as their father, Dick Cheney, has publicly voiced support for same-sex marriage and also believes it should be decided on a state-by-state basis. The sisters' disagreement highlights the ongoing debate over same-sex marriage in the United States.
