SUMMARY:

In a rare public display of sibling disagreement, Mary Cheney, the openly lesbian daughter of former Vice President Dick Cheney, took to Facebook to criticize her sister Elizabeth's stance on same-sex marriage. Mary Cheney, who has been married to her partner Heather Poe since 2012, expressed her love for her sister while strongly disagreeing with her views. Elizabeth Cheney, a Wyoming Senate candidate, believes that same-sex marriage should be decided on a state-by-state basis, rather than by judges or legislators. Mary Cheney countered that "freedom means freedom for everyone" and that all families deserve the same rights and protections. Their father, former Vice President Dick Cheney, has publicly voiced support for same-sex marriage, also advocating for a state-by-state decision. The public disagreement highlights the complexities of family dynamics and the ongoing debate over same-sex marriage.

END OF SUMMARY.
