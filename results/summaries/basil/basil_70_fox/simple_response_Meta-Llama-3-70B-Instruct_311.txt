The address was heavy on populist themes, reprising objectives from his 2016 campaign on trade, immigration, and national security. Trump's presidency is seen as a return to power for the people, and he vowed never to let them down.

The moral framing of the article portrays Trump's presidency as a necessary correction to the status quo, where the powerful few have exploited the many. Trump's appeal to patriotism and his promise to put America first are presented as a unifying force, opposed to the divisions and prejudices that have plagued the country. The article suggests that Trump's presidency marks a new era of action and results, after years of empty talk and corruption.
