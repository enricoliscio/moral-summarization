SUMMARY: Senator Marco Rubio, a Republican presidential candidate, outlined his foreign policy approach, emphasizing three key pillars: American strength, economic protection, and moral clarity. Rubio prioritizes funding and strengthening the military to oppose international violations and protect smaller nations from subjugation. He pledges to support economic and political freedom, reinforce alliances, and resist invasion of other countries' territories, including cyberspace and outer space. Rubio also commits to advancing the rights of the vulnerable and persecuted, demonstrating a desire to see their suffering alleviated and their freedom achieved. Rubio's vision underscores the importance of maintaining America's core values and moral clarity on the world stage.

END OF SUMMARY.
