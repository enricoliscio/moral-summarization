SUMMARY: Senator Marco Rubio, a Republican from Florida and presidential candidate for 2016, intends to outline his approach to foreign policy, which includes three main pillars: strengthening America, protecting the American economy, and preserving the moral clarity of American values. Mr. Rubio prioritizes funding and strengthening the military to oppose violations of international laws, including invasions of other countries. He promises to support the spread of freedom and reinforce alliances while resisting the subjugation of smaller nations by larger powers. Mr. Rubio also commits to maintaining foreign assistance programs and advancing the rights of the vulnerable, such as persecuted religious minorities, showcasing his desire to address the suffering of afflicted peoples and his commitment to moral clarity in foreign policy.

END OF SUMMARY.
