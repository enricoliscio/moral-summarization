SUMMARY: Senator Marco Rubio, a Republican from Florida and 2016 presidential candidate, outlined his approach to foreign policy in a speech before the Council on Foreign Relations. Rubio's doctrine, focusing on preserving America's moral clarity and core values, includes three main pillars: strengthening America's military might, protecting the American economy in a globalized world, and promoting economic and political freedom worldwide. Rubio pledges to adequately fund the military and use American power to oppose violations of international laws and to protect smaller nations from subjugation by larger powers. He also commits to maintaining transparent foreign assistance programs and advancing the rights of the vulnerable, demonstrating a desire to hear their cries and see their suffering. Rubio's speech highlights his commitment to resisting aggression, reinforcing alliances, and preserving the moral high ground in a complex global landscape.

END OF SUMMARY.
