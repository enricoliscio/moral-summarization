SUMMARY: Senator Marco Rubio, a Republican from Florida and presidential candidate, outlined his approach to foreign policy in a speech before the Council on Foreign Relations. Rubio's foreign policy approach consists of three main pillars: ensuring American strength, protecting the American economy in a globalized world, and preserving America's moral values. Rubio emphasizes the need to "adequately fund" the military and use American power to oppose violations of international laws and economic disruptions caused by invasions. He also expresses his support for the spread of economic and political freedom, reinforcing alliances, and resisting subjugation by large powers. Rubio aims to maintain a commitment to foreign assistance and advance the rights of vulnerable groups, positioning the US as a supporter of freedom and human rights. Rubio's stance highlights his focus on a strong military and a proactive role for America in global affairs.
END OF SUMMARY
