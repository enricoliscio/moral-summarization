SUMMARY: As a presidential candidate, Senator Marco Rubio has outlined his approach to foreign policy, emphasizing three main pillars: American strength, economic protection, and moral clarity. Mr. Rubio promises to "adequately fund" the military and use American power to defend international waters, airspace, cyberspace, and outer space, demonstrating a defense hawk stance. He also commits to supporting the spread of economic and political freedom, reinforcing alliances, resisting subjugation by large powers, and advancing the rights of vulnerable groups. Mr. Rubio's foreign policy vision highlights the use of American power for global stability and the protection of human rights, reflecting his interpretation of moral clarity in foreign affairs. END OF SUMMARY.
