SUMMARY:

Senator Marco Rubio, a 2016 presidential candidate, has outlined his approach to foreign policy, which prioritizes American strength, economic protection, and moral clarity. Rubio's doctrine emphasizes the importance of adequately funding the military, even in times of peace and stability. He vows to use American power to oppose violations of international waters, airspace, cyberspace, and outer space, and to promote economic and political freedom globally. Rubio's vision also includes reinforcing alliances, resisting large powers' subjugation of smaller neighbors, and advancing the rights of vulnerable groups, such as women and religious minorities. His approach is rooted in a strong moral foundation, emphasizing the importance of hearing the cries and seeing the suffering of afflicted peoples around the world. Rubio's doctrine presents a clear and principled stance on foreign policy, distinguishing him from other presidential candidates.

END OF SUMMARY.
