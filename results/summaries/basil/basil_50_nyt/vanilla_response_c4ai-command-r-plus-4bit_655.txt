SUMMARY: Susan E. Rice, President Obama's national security adviser, criticized Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu for his plans to address Congress regarding a potential nuclear deal with Iran. Rice stated that Netanyahu's actions have damaged the relationship between the US and Israel by injecting partisanship. The speech was arranged without consulting the White House, and Obama has said he will not meet with Netanyahu during his visit. Vice President Biden and Secretary of State Kerry also have conflicting schedules. The controversy has sparked anonymous sniping and finger-pointing, with top administration officials expressing their displeasure. Netanyahu has stated that he is making the trip to prevent a nuclear agreement with Iran. Rice emphasized the importance of an unquestionably strong relationship between the US and Israel, regardless of political seasons or parties.

END OF SUMMARY.
