Biden aimed to rally the Democratic base after Obama's underwhelming debate performance, interrupting Ryan repeatedly and challenging him on Medicare and tax policy. Biden raised Romney's controversial comments about government benefits recipients, while Ryan defended him with a humanizing story. The Obama campaign praised Biden's passion, while Republicans criticized his behavior as condescending and over-the-top. Biden's performance likely energized Democrats, but it is unclear how it impacted undecided voters. The debate highlighted the differences in style and substance between the two campaigns, with Democrats focusing on the middle class and Republicans emphasizing substance and composure. Biden's approach may foreshadow Obama's strategy in the upcoming presidential debates.
