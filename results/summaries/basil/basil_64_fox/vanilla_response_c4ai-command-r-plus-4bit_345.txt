SUMMARY: Supreme Court Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg faced intense backlash after criticizing Donald Trump, calling him a "faker" and unqualified to be president. Trump responded by calling for her resignation, stating that her comments were inappropriate for a Supreme Court justice. Republican leaders, Democratic lawmakers, and mainstream media outlets joined in the criticism, expressing concern over the politicization of the Supreme Court. Ginsburg's comments were seen as a breach of the Code of Conduct for U.S. judges. However, Senator Bernie Sanders offered qualified support, agreeing with Ginsburg's assessment of Trump.

END OF SUMMARY.
