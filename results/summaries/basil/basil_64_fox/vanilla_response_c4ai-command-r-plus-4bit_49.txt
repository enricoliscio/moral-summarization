SUMMARY: Supreme Court Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg faced intense backlash from both sides of the political spectrum for her comments criticizing Donald Trump, the presumptive GOP nominee for president. Ginsburg called Trump a "faker" and questioned his qualifications, sparking outrage from political figures, editorial writers, and Trump himself. Trump demanded Ginsburg's resignation, while Republican leaders and some Democrats criticized her comments as inappropriate and damaging to the impartiality of the Supreme Court. Ginsburg's remarks also drew criticism from mainstream media outlets, citing the Code of Conduct for U.S. judges. However, Senator Bernie Sanders offered qualified support for Ginsburg's comments, agreeing with her assessment of Trump as an opportunist and a liar. Ginsburg's comments sparked a debate about the appropriateness of a Supreme Court justice expressing political views publicly.
END OF SUMMARY.
