SUMMARY: A video surfaced of Rep. Bob Etheridge (D-N.C.) responding aggressively to a reporter's question about his support for the "Obama agenda." Etheridge appeared irritated, asking the reporter's identity and knocking the camera to the ground. The incident sparked criticism and discussion about the ethics of anonymous inflammatory interviews. Mediaite argued that Etheridge's response might be a result of this new brand of journalism. The DNC and Etheridge himself issued apologies for the incident, with Etheridge expressing regret and reaffirming his commitment to civil public discourse.

END OF SUMMARY.
