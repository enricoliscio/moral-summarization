However, after facing backlash, Lauten apologized for her hurtful words, acknowledging that she had judged the girls unfairly. She admitted that her reaction was wrong and that she would not have wanted to be judged similarly as a teenager. Lauten expressed regret for the hurt and offense she caused, and pledged to learn and grow from the experience. Her apology highlighted the importance of respecting others, particularly young people, and avoiding judgmental behavior. By acknowledging her mistake, Lauten demonstrated a commitment to personal growth and a willingness to make amends.
