SUMMARY: Despite their different circles in New York's ultrarich community, Michael Bloomberg and Donald Trump have had limited yet cordial interactions over the years. They played golf together in 2007 and Bloomberg appeared on Trump's show, "The Apprentice." Bloomberg's daughter Georgina is also friends with Trump's daughter Ivanka. Trump praised Bloomberg on Twitter during his time as mayor and congratulated Georgina on her equestrian win. However, their relationship soured after Trump's presidential candidacy, with Bloomberg exploring his own independent bid to block Trump. Trump has since attacked Bloomberg, questioning his wealth and the stability of his company. Their relationship highlights the complex web of connections among the wealthy and influential in New York City.
END OF SUMMARY.
